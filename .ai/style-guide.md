# Style Guide

Complements the AI Profile with formatting and structural conventions.

## Document Structure

- Use headings (##, ###) for hierarchy
- Short paragraphs (2–4 sentences)
- Bullet points for lists
- Tables for structured data

## Worldbuilding Documents

- **history**: factual, neutral tone
- **geography**: descriptive, spatial
- **people/cultures**: ethnographic, descriptive
- **cosmology/religion**: structured belief systems
- **cosmology/myths**: narrative, in-world voice when appropriate; always include penetration (P1–P5)

## Links

- Use `[[wikilinks]]` for cross-references between documents
- Link to related concepts, not every occurrence

## Language

- Portuguese (Pt-BR) for worldbuilding content
- English acceptable for technical/architectural notes
