# Minor NPCs of Black Hollow

---

## **The Hollow Hearth Tavern**

### **Gared "One-Eye" Marsh – Tavernkeeper**

- **Role:** Runs the barely-functioning tavern, former mercenary
- **Appearance:** Burly man in his 50s, graying beard, leather eyepatch. Wears a stained apron over old mercenary leathers.
- **Personality:** Gruff but fair, keeps order with an iron fist
- **Notable:** Lost his eye in the Blackened Company's last stand
- **Quote:** *"Pay your tab or sleep in the mud. Your choice."*

### **Anna Blackwood – Serving Girl**

- **Role:** Waitress, unofficial messenger for The Veil
- **Appearance:** Young woman with dark hair tied back, quick smile that doesn't reach her eyes. Carries hidden daggers in her boots.
- **Personality:** Sharp-witted, observant, knows everyone's business
- **Notable:** Has a network of street urchins who gather information
- **Quote:** *"Secrets are worth more than silver these days."*

---

## **Joan's Supply Shop**

### **Joan Graves – Shopkeeper**

- **Role:** Runs the town's only general store
- **Appearance:** Middle-aged woman with silver-streaked black hair, perpetually ink-stained fingers. Wears practical clothes with many hidden pockets.
- **Personality:** Shrewd, cautious, maintains detailed records
- **Notable:** Has hidden stockpiles of rare goods
- **Quote:** *"Everything has a price. The question is: can you afford it?"*

### **Tam "Quick-Fingers" – Stock Boy**

- **Role:** Mila's assistant, secret fence for stolen goods
- **Appearance:** Scrawny teenager with perpetually messy brown hair, darting eyes, and quick hands. Clothes too big for his frame.
- **Personality:** Nervous, fidgety, but reliable for certain services
- **Notable:** Works with Marla's thieves guild
- **Quote:** *"I might know someone who knows someone..."*

---

## **The Streets & Markets**

### **Old Beth – Market Herbalist**

- **Role:** Sells herbs, poultices, and "folk remedies"
- **Appearance:** Elderly woman wrapped in layers of shawls, cloudy blue eyes that seem to see too much. Fingers stained green from herbs.
- **Personality:** Kindly grandmother type with a hint of mystery
- **Notable:** May have connections to [[ithiriel-keeper-of-the-garden|Morvanna]]
- **Quote:** *"The old ways still work, dearie. Sometimes better than the new."*

### **Rook – Street Informant**

- **Role:** Beggar, unofficial town crier, information broker
- **Appearance:** Hunched figure in patchwork clothes, face hidden under a deep hood. Missing several teeth, showing them in a knowing grin.
- **Personality:** Seemingly simple-minded, but sees everything
- **Notable:** Reports to both The Veil and Aldric
- **Quote:** *"Penny for the poor? I've got stories worth gold..."*

---

## **The Docks & Warehouses**

### **Durnan "Salt-Beard" – Warehouse Master**

- **Role:** Manages the few remaining storage houses
- **Appearance:** Tall, broad-shouldered man with a white-crusted beard. Wears a heavy ring of keys at his belt and carries a weathered ledger.
- **Personality:** Practical, no-nonsense, values order
- **Notable:** Keeps detailed records of shipments
- **Quote:** *"Everything in its place, and a place for everything."*

### **The Twins (Gil & Gus) – Dock Workers**

- **Role:** Loading/unloading, unofficial muscle
- **Appearance:** Identical massive men with shaved heads and matching anchor tattoos. Only Gil has a scar above his right eye.
- **Personality:** Identical twins, finish each other's sentences
- **Notable:** Former circus strongmen, now work for highest bidder
- **Quote:** *"Two heads are better than one, and four arms better than two!"*

---

## **The Outskirts**

### **Widow Crane – Farmstead Owner**

- **Role:** Maintains one of the few producing farms
- **Appearance:** Weather-beaten woman in her 60s, always wearing a broad-brimmed hat. Carries a woodsman's axe at her belt.
- **Personality:** Tough, independent, distrustful of strangers
- **Notable:** Her land borders the Withered Thicket
- **Quote:** *"The forest takes more than it gives these days."*

### **Reed the Hunter**

- **Role:** Provides game meat to the tavern, knows the safe paths
- **Appearance:** Lean man in worn leathers, face weathered beyond his years. Carries a distinctive recurve bow with bone inlays.
- **Personality:** Quiet, observant, slightly paranoid
- **Notable:** Has seen strange things in the woods
- **Quote:** *"Some tracks you don't follow. Some sounds you don't investigate."*

---

## **The Gilded Dawn Post**

### **Sergeant Voss – Guard Captain**

- **Role:** Leads the small contingent of guards
- **Appearance:** Clean-shaven man in well-maintained but worn Gilded Dawn armor. Perfect posture betrays his military background.
- **Personality:** Professional, by-the-book, slightly overwhelmed
- **Notable:** Not preset at the start - needs upgrades
- **Quote:** *"We maintain order. That's all we can do for now."*

### **Mari "Swift" – Messenger**

- **Role:** Carries messages between Black Hollow and Duskport
- **Appearance:** Athletic young woman with short-cropped red hair, wearing light leather armor with the Gilded Dawn insignia.
- **Personality:** Athletic, cheerful despite circumstances
- **Notable:** Knows all the safe routes and shortcuts
- **Quote:** *"Two days to Duskport? That's only if you take the slow road!"*

---

## **Xerxes, the Cat**

- **Role:** The mysterious cat that seems to be known by everyone in town. Actually a **Fey Cat** — servo de [[ithiriel-keeper-of-the-garden|Morvanna]].
- **Appearance:** A completely black cat. Perto da cabana de Morvanna, a presença Fey é mais óbvia: olhos brilham com luz própria, pelo parece mudar de tom com a luz, movimentos antinaturalmente precisos.
- **Personality:** Em Black Hollow: "just a normal black cat." Na cabana: **fala**. Frases curtas, crípticas, tom seco. Julga. Decide quem entra. Só deixa **um por vez**.
- **Notable:** Mora na taverna há anos. **Implicação:** Morvanna sabe muito mais sobre Black Hollow e os PCs do que deveria. Tudo que Xerxes viu, ela viu.
- **Quote (Black Hollow):** *"Miau."*
- **Quote (Cabana):** *"Um. Só um."*
