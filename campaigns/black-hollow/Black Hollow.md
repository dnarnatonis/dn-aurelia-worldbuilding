# **Black Hollow – Core Elements Summary**

## **1. The Hamlet of Black Hollow**  

**Population:** ~200 (formerly ~800-1000)  
**Geography:**  

- Located on the **northern frontier of the Aurelian Empire**, near the **Sea of the North**
- Surrounded by **dense, corrupted forests (The Withered Thicket)** - corrupted by the Hag of the Corrupted Forest's influence
- Built upon the **ruins of an old noble estate**
- **Two days away from Duskport** (closest town) by coach

**Mood & Themes:**  

- **Gothic despair, slow decay, and looming dread**
- **A town that remembers too much, where hope is as rare as gold**
- **The dead do not sleep easily here**

## **4. The Corruption & First Adventure**

### **The Dark History**

- **The Hollow Lord** - Last ruler, sealed away but not dead
- **The Hag of the Withered Thicket** - His former lover, now twisted
- **The Manor Dungeon** contains:
  - Bandits and cultists fighting over power
  - A mad prophet with the lord's secret
  - A necromancer awakening ancient evils

### **"The Hollow Beckons" - First Adventure**

- Players arrive with Gilded Dawn writ to stabilize town
- Strange occurrences: disturbed graves, prophetic beggar, missing scavengers
- Culminates in manor ruins conflict between treasure hunters and cultists

## **5. Future Development Paths**

- **Town Restoration:** Can become new stronghold of order
- **Complete Collapse:** May become another wilderness ruin
- **The Hollow Lord:** His return is inevitable - freed, stopped, or transformed?
