# Master Plot

## **The Sentence**

**The Gravedigger wants to establish a stable psychic connection with the imprisoned Aboleth to learn its immortality secrets before her cultists' increasingly desperate attempts at communication accidentally break its dimensional prison, and is having difficulty achieving this using ritualistic experiments because each attempt risks either alerting the local authorities or fracturing the ancient seals that keep the entity contained.**

### Breaking Down The Sentence

- **Someone**: The Gravedigger - a Black Dragon masquerading as a respected academic
- **Wants Something**: To establish stable psychic communication with the Aboleth and learn its immortality secrets
- **Badly**: This knowledge is crucial for her plans of transcending even draconic longevity
- **By a Specific Time**: Before her cultists' increasingly desperate attempts accidentally break the prison
- **Having Difficulty**: Each attempt is a delicate balance of power and control
- **Using Something**: Ritualistic experiments and her network of cultists
- **Because of Reasons**: The attempts risk either exposing her true nature to local authorities or accidentally breaking the dimensional seals

### Campaign Tensions Created

1. **Control vs Progress**: She must control her cultists' enthusiasm while still using their help
2. **Risk vs Reward**: Each attempt at communication brings both knowledge and danger
3. **Time vs Caution**: Racing against her own followers' potential mistakes
4. **Knowledge vs Safety**: The more she learns, the more unstable the situation becomes
5. **Secrecy vs Power**: Balancing her need for resources with maintaining her cover

### The Hollow Lord Connection

The Gravedigger's network extends beyond the cult to include her past collaboration with Lord Matthias Blackthorne (now the Hollow Lord). This partnership provided her with:

- **Perfected Revenant Creation**: The methods she now uses to create the Moon Cultist Prophets and other undead servants
- **Regional Undead Network**: Intelligent undead agents throughout the region who report to her
- **Necromantic Knowledge**: Advanced understanding of soul binding and undeath

The Hollow Lord's current state as a mindless abomination serves The Gravedigger's purposes perfectly:

- He guards the deeper secrets of the ancient ruins without being able to reveal them
- His corruption keeps the manor sealed and forgotten
- His presence provides a testing ground for her undead servants
- His research continues to benefit her network while he remains oblivious to being used

This connection creates additional campaign tensions:

- **Past Collaborations**: The consequences of The Gravedigger's past deals coming back to haunt her
- **Lost Knowledge**: Crucial information trapped in the Hollow Lord's fractured mind
- **Competing Interests**: Managing her network while preventing discovery of her methods
- **The Manor's Secrets**: Balancing access to the ancient ruins with keeping the Hollow Lord contained

## Core Elements

### The Hollow Lord (Boss)
- **Location**: Family crypt beneath the Fallen Manor
- **Role**: Failed alchemist who became a mindless necromancer
- **Threat**: Creates revenants and undead creatures
- **Connection**: Former partner of The Gravedigger in necromantic research
- **Weakness**: Destroying his ritual components prevents regeneration

### The Gravedigger's Network
- He guards the deeper secrets of the ancient ruins without being able to reveal them
- His cultists operate from hidden locations throughout the region
- They seek to claim artifacts and knowledge from the ancient city below
- The network uses the manor's ruins as a base of operations
