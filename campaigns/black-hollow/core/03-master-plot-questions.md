# Master Plot - The 5 W Analysis

## Stage 1: Obtaining Requirements

### The Ithilnorith [Failed]

- **What:** Ancient chronicle with forbidden knowledge
- **Who:** Originally targeted by cultists
- **Where:** Black Hollow cemetery (Prophet's Crypt)
- **When:** Already attempted and failed
- **Why:** Contains crucial Aboleth knowledge

### The Prophet

- **What:** Imprisoned madre with powerful visions
- **Who:** Cultists maintain her prison
- **Where:** Dragon Cult sarcophagus in manor
- **When:** Currently accessible
- **Why:** Needed to locate other artifacts

### Aboleth Artifacts

- **What:** Ancient dimensional gate disguised as stone door
- **Who:** Protected by multiple layers of guardians
- **Where:** Deep beneath the manor, leading to The City of the Gods
- **When:** After securing access through lower levels
- **Why:** Direct connection to pre-divine human civilization and Aboleth influence

### The City of the Gods

- **What:** Ancient pre-divine human settlement ruins
- **Who:** Protected by ancient defenses and current denizens
- **Where:** Deep beneath the manor, accessed through The Mines
- **When:** Must be explored before reaching the gate
- **Why:** Houses the dimensional gate to Aboleth's prison

### The Dimensional Gate

- **What:** Portal to the Aboleth's prison
- **Who:** Created by ancient humans to contain the Aboleth
- **Where:** In a special chamber within The City of the Gods
- **When:** Can only be accessed after navigating the city
- **Why:** Direct access point to the Aboleth's knowledge

*Key Connection:* The Sage's discovered text "The City of the Gods" provides crucial historical context about this ancient human settlement from before the Age of Heroes.

### Research Materials

- **What:** Pre-divine civilization records
- **Who:** Academic institutions, private collections
- **Where:** Libraries, archives, ruins
- **When:** Ongoing collection
- **Why:** Build scholarly cover while gathering real data

## Stage 2: Building & Preparation

### Black Hollow Base

- **What:** Convert town into research center
- **Who:** Cultists, Black Sword Company
- **Where:** Centered on manor, spread through town
- **When:** Currently in progress
- **Why:** Need controlled environment for experiments

### Research Network

- **What:** Network of scholars and cultists
- **Who:** Recruited academics and converts
- **Where:** Throughout academic institutions
- **When:** Years of careful recruitment
- **Why:** Need skilled researchers and test subjects

### Ritual Sites

- **What:** Places of power for experiments
- **Who:** Cultists prepare and maintain
- **Where:** Manor and backup locations
- **When:** As discovered and prepared
- **Why:** Need controlled environments for tests

## Stage 3: Testing

### Cultist Transformations

- **What:** Attempts at partial immortality
- **Who:** Willing cultist volunteers
- **Where:** Manor's secure chambers
- **When:** After proper preparation
- **Why:** Test theories before personal attempt

### Artifact Combinations

- **What:** Testing power interactions
- **Who:** Trusted inner circle
- **Where:** Specially prepared chambers
- **When:** As artifacts are gathered
- **Why:** Understand how powers combine

### Final Preparations

- **What:** Full-scale transformation test
- **Who:** Chosen cultist champion
- **Where:** Primary ritual site
- **When:** When all elements align
- **Why:** Final verification before personal attempt

## Stage 4: The Final Goal

### Aboleth Contact

- **What:** Direct communication ritual
- **Who:** The Gravedigger personally
- **Where:** Prepared primary site
- **When:** At moment of perfect alignment
- **Why:** Gain final knowledge needed

### Transcendence

- **What:** Personal transformation ritual
- **Who:** The Gravedigger
- **Where:** Most powerful ritual site
- **When:** Immediately after contact
- **Why:** Achieve true immortality

### Contingencies

- **What:** Backup plans and escape routes
- **Who:** Trusted lieutenants
- **Where:** Multiple backup sites
- **When:** If primary plan fails
- **Why:** Ensure goal can still be achieved
