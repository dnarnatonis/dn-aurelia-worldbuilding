# Master Plot: The Path to Transcendence

## The Core Sentence

The Gravedigger, a dragon masquerading as an academic, seeks to achieve true immortality through the Aboleth's forbidden knowledge, while maintaining her deceptions within both the academic world and the Cabal.

## Plot Structure

### Stage 1: Obtaining Requirements

**Key Items & Knowledge Required:**

1. The Ithilnorith (Ancient Chronicle) [Failed - Now with PCs]
2. The Prophet's Visions
   - Currently secured in Dragon Cult sarcophagus
   - Essential for locating other artifacts
   - Must be carefully managed to prevent madness
3. The Path to the Aboleth
   - The City of the Gods (ancient human ruin beneath manor)
   - Houses the dimensional gate to Aboleth's prison
   - Multiple security layers protect the descent:
     * Level 1: Kobold and Goblin territories [Current]
     * Level 2: The Undercroft (manor's original basement)
       - Family crypt with noble history
       - Evidence of lord's descent into obsession
       - Entrance to excavation tunnel
     * Deeper Levels: The City of the Gods
       - Pre-divine human settlement ruins
       - Ancient technological/magical achievements
       - Path to the dimensional gate
     * The Gate: Portal to Aboleth's prison
4. Pre-Divine Research Materials
   - Ancient texts (including "The City of the Gods" chronicle)
   - Archaeological evidence
   - Lord's excavation journals and notes
   - Scholarly works about pre-divine civilizations
5. Ritual Sites
   - The City of the Gods ruins (preparation site)
   - Dimensional gate chamber
   - Backup locations with similar resonance

**Expected Adventures:**

1. Securing the Prophet [In Progress]
2. Accessing the lower manor levels
   - Navigate the Undercroft
   - Discover lord's research
   - Find excavation entrance
3. Exploring The City of the Gods
   - Map the ancient ruins
   - Understand pre-divine technology
   - Locate the gate chamber
4. Opening the dimensional gate
5. Reaching the Aboleth's prison

### Stage 2: Building & Preparation

**Required Elements:**

1. Black Hollow Power Base
   - Control local authorities
   - Convert manor into primary research hub
   - Secure access to underground levels
   - Create supply networks
2. Cultist Research Network
   - Recruit scholars and experts
   - Train ritual participants
   - Establish communication systems
3. Ritual Site Preparation
   - Stabilize dimensional gate
   - Prepare City of the Gods ritual chamber
   - Secure backup sites
4. Knowledge Transfer Preparations
   - Develop containment methods
   - Create backup vessels
   - Test preservation techniques

**Expected Adventures:**

1. Manor fortification
2. Network expansion
3. Site preparation
4. Vessel creation

### Stage 3: Establishing Contact

**Critical Steps:**

1. The Prophet's Connection
   - Use Prophet's dream-walking abilities
   - Two cultist handlers monitoring dreams
   - Establish initial telepathic link to Aboleth
   - Risk of madness from contact
   - Careful documentation of visions

2. Dream Interpretation
   - Cultist handlers interpreting Prophet's dreams
   - Mapping mental pathways to Aboleth
   - Identifying prison's psychic defenses
   - Recording Aboleth's responses
   - Protecting Prophet from complete corruption

3. Failed Previous Attempt [Manor Lord]
   - Tried direct contact without Prophet
   - Resulted in transformation/corruption
   - Valuable lesson about need for intermediary
   - Proof of Aboleth's power
   - Warning about unprotected contact

**Expected Adventures:**

1. Disrupting the dream sessions
2. Rescuing/securing the Prophet
3. Dealing with corrupted cultist handlers

### Stage 4: The Final Goal

**Culmination:**

1. Aboleth Contact
   - Through Prophet's established connection
   - Protected by ritual preparations
   - Direct knowledge transfer
   - Managed corruption risk

2. Transcendence Ritual
   - Using Prophet's guidance
   - Perfect timing required
   - Multiple backup plans
   - Contingencies for interference

3. Ascension
   - Beyond dragon limitations
   - True immortality
   - Controlled transformation

## Timeline Pressure

- Cabal growing suspicious of research
- Green Dragon's increasing attention
- Divine forces sensing disturbance
- Other parties seeking artifacts
- Prophet's deteriorating condition

## Complications

- Players possess the Ithilnorith
- Failed experiments drawing attention
- Multiple competing factions
- Limited window for ritual completion
- Maintaining multiple deceptions

## Adaptation Points

The plan must be flexible enough to account for:

1. Loss of key items to players
2. Failed experiments providing new data
3. Alternative paths to knowledge
4. Backup ritual sites
5. Different methods of achieving immortality

- Stage 2: In progress, manor conversion ongoing
- Stage 3: Early experiments yielding data
- Stage 4: Preparations beginning
