# Campaign Structure & Adventure Progression

The **leveling structure** ensures players begin as desperate survivors in a dying hamlet and gradually establish themselves as local heroes. This progression allows **Black Hollow's fate to evolve with the characters**, focusing on rebuilding hope in a place of decay.

## **Level Progression & Milestone Adventures**

Each tier follows a **specific narrative arc**, gradually increasing **stakes, magic, and influence.**

### **Levels 1-4: Local Heroes (Black Hollow's Survival)**

- **Core Theme:** Rebuilding a dying settlement, surviving the wilderness, and uncovering the first hints of an ancient horror.
- **Magic Level:** Limited to **potions, scrolls, and a few uncommon permanent items.**
- **Enemies:**
  - Bandits and cultists
  - Undead and minor supernatural threats
  - Wild beasts (wolves, giant spiders)
  - The corrupted wilderness itself
- **Key Adventures:**
  1. **"The Hollow Call" (Level 1)** ✓
     - Survived ambush by Black Sword Company on the road
     - Recovered the Ithilnorith, an ancient sealed chronicle
     - Met Zoraia who warned of the book's dual nature
     - Established first connections in the Hollow Hearth
     - Found Ring of Mind Shielding with the Gravedigger's mark
     - Formed alliances with Aldric, Liriel, and Mari
     - Negotiated trade deal with The Veil (Marla)
  
  2. **"The Prophet's Crypt" (Level 2)** ✓
     - Investigated disappearances in the old cemetery
     - Confronted and defeated corrupted Madre Eudokia
     - Discovered Dragon Cult's ancient sarcophagus
     - Uncovered first hints of the Gravedigger's influence
     - Found evidence of cult activity in Black Hollow
     - Captured and interrogated cultist
     - Recovered valuable church artifacts
     - Revealed manor as cult base of operations

  3. **"The Ruined Manor" (Level 3)** 🔄
     - Negotiated alliance with kobold tribe
     - Retrieved Lumenaris (+1 Longsword)
     - Discovered ancient texts:
       - "Auriana's Sorrow" (Religious)
       - "The City of the Gods" (Historical)
     - Found Silas's dead drop with sending stone
     - Convinced dragon wyrmling Nyxis to leave
     - Currently navigating goblin tensions
     - Still to explore:
       - Deeper manor levels
       - Cultist operations
       - Connection to Aboleth
       - Fate of transformed lord

  4. **"The Excavation" (Level 3)**
     - [Adventure details pending based on player choices]

👉 *By Level 4, the town is no longer on the verge of collapse, but deeper threats loom beneath the surface.*

### **Adventure Rewards**

**Individual Treasure (per encounter):**

- Levels 1-4: 3d6 (10) GP average

**Quest Rewards (per major milestone):**

- 2d4 × 100 (500) GP
- 1d4 - 1 magic items

*Notable Items Acquired:*

- Ithilnorith (Sealed Chronicle state)
- Ring of Mind Shielding (Gravedigger's mark)
- Lumenaris (+1 Longsword, Dragon Order relic)
- Sending stone (from Silas)
- Ancient texts (2)
- Valuable church artifacts

### **Levels 5-10: Heroes of the Realm (Regional Influence)**

- **Core Theme:** The players **become power players**, influencing **Black Hollow's fate, the surrounding region, and even the empire.**
- **Magic Level:** Uncommon and rare magic items, **significant spell upgrades (fireball, teleportation, scrying).**
- **Enemies:** Giants, hydras, drow assassins, demons, devils, and supernatural horrors.
- **Key Adventures:**
  - **The Silver Host Conflict:** The **breakaway kingdom sees Black Hollow as a strategic foothold.** Will the players **ally, resist, or manipulate?**
  - **The Hollow Lord's Awakening:** **An ancient force stirs**, threatening **everything they've built.**
  - **Imperial Interest:** The **Aurelian Empire** starts noticing **Black Hollow's growing significance.**
  - **Final Showdown:** The **players decide the fate of the town, the region, and its people.**

👉 *By Level 10, Black Hollow will either be a **thriving stronghold, a corrupt haven, or a ruin lost to darkness.***
