### Skills

Perception +6, Stealth +7, Survival +6

### Equipment

Studded leather armor, longbow, Scimitar (+1)

### Other Characteristics

**Senses** Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 16
**Languages** Goblin, Abyssal
**Challenge** 3 (700 XP)
**Proficiency Bonus** +2

### Actions

**Multiattack.** Warlord Grikzash makes two attacks, using shortsword or longbow in any combination.

**Scimitar (+1).** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 6 (1d6 + 4) piercing damage, plus 10 (3d6) piercing damage if the attack was made with advantage.

**Longbow.** *Ranged Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. *Hit:* 7 (1d8 + 3) piercing damage, plus 10 (3d6) piercing damage if the attack was made with advantage.

### Bonus Actions

**Aim.** Warlord Grikzash has advantage on the next attack roll it makes during the current turn.

### Reactions

**Uncanny Dodge.** *Trigger:* Warlord Grikzash is hit by an attack roll. *Response:* The scout captain halves the damage (round down) it takes from that attack.
