# Kobold Tribe

## Nyxis, Kobold Wyrmling

**White Dragon Wyrmling**  
*Medium Dragon (Chromatic), Chaotic Evil*

**Armor Class** 16  
**Hit Points** 32 (5d8 + 10)  
**Speed** 30 ft., burrow 15 ft., fly 60 ft., swim 30 ft.  
**Initiative** +2 (12)

| Ability | Score | Modifier | Save |
|---------|--------|----------|------|
| STR | 14 | +2 | +2 |
| DEX | 10 | +0 | +2 |
| CON | 14 | +2 | +2 |
| INT | 5 | -3 | -3 |
| WIS | 10 | +0 | +2 |
| CHA | 11 | +0 | +0 |

**Saving Throws** STR +2, DEX +2, CON +2, WIS +2  
**Skills** Perception +4, Stealth +2  
**Damage Immunities** Cold  
**Senses** Blindsight 10 ft., Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 14  
**Languages** Draconic  
**Challenge** 2 (450 XP, Proficiency Bonus +2)

### Traits

***Ice Walk.*** The dragon can move across and climb icy surfaces without needing to make an ability check. Additionally, difficult terrain composed of ice or snow doesn't cost it extra movement.

### Actions

***Multiattack.*** The dragon makes two Rend attacks.

***Rend.*** *Melee Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 6 (1d8 + 2) slashing damage plus 2 (1d4) cold damage.

***Cold Breath (Recharge 5–6).*** The dragon exhales freezing air in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 12 Constitution saving throw, taking 22 (5d8) cold damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

| CHA | 8 | -1 | -1 |

**Gear** Daggers (3)  
**Senses** Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 8  
**Languages** Common, Draconic

### Actions

## Elder Vissk, Kobold Draconic Priest

**Small Dragon, Lawful Evil**

**Armor Class** 13 (scale mail vestments)  
**Hit Points** 11 (2d6 + 4)  
**Speed** 30 ft.  
**Initiative** +1 (11) 

| Ability | Score | Modifier | Save |
|---------|--------|----------|------|
| STR | 8 | -1 | -1 |
| DEX | 12 | +1 | +1 |
| CON | 14 | +2 | +2 |
| INT | 10 | +0 | +0 |
| WIS | 15 | +2 | +4 |
| CHA | 13 | +1 | +1 |

**Skills** Religion +2, Arcana +2, Medicine +4  
**Gear** Scale mail vestments, dragon-tooth staff, draconic holy symbol  
**Senses** Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 12  
**Languages** Common, Draconic, Abyssal
**Challenge** 1/4 (50 XP, Proficiency Bonus +2)

### Traits

***Pack Tactics.*** The kobold has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the kobold's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.

***Sunlight Sensitivity.*** While in sunlight, the kobold has disadvantage on ability checks and attack rolls.

***Draconic Devotion.*** When Elder Vissk casts a spell while within 30 feet of Nyxis, the spell is cast as if using a spell slot one level higher.

### Actions

***Dragon-Tooth Staff.*** *Melee Attack:* +1 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 2 (1d6 - 1) bludgeoning damage plus 2 (1d4) cold damage.

***Frost Bolt.*** *Ranged Attack:* +4 to hit, range 60 ft., one target. *Hit:* 7 (2d6) cold damage.

***Spellcasting.*** Elder Vissk casts one of the following spells, using Wisdom as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 12):

At Will: *mending*, *thaumaturgy* (draconic-themed effects)

1st-level (3 slots): *ice knife*, *shield of faith*, *frost fingers*

### Bonus Actions

***Draconic Blessing (1/Day).*** Elder Vissk can cast one of the following spells without using a spell slot: *bless*, *healing word*, or *protection from evil and good* (targeting only dragon creatures).

### Reactions

***Draconic Shield.*** When a creature Elder Vissk can see within 30 feet takes damage, Elder Vissk can reduce that damage by 1d4 + 2.
