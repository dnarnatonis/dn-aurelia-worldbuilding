# Tumba do Oráculo

> Azulejos de mármore cobrem o chão e as paredes, embora muitos estejam rachados ou quebrados, revelando pedra bruta por baixo. Quatro colunas imponentes sustentam o teto nos cantos da sala, cada uma entalhada com símbolos dracônicos que parecem brilhar levemente na penumbra. Dois círculos mágicos intrincados foram recentemente gravados no chão de mármore, suas linhas arcanas cintilando com uma luz prateada tênue - os locais onde os cultistas agora realizam seus rituais. Um sarcophagus de mármore, facilmente com sete pés de comprimento, repousa no centro da sala, entre os círculos. O caixão está entalhado com imagens de dragões majestosos e símbolos da antiga Ordem do Dragão, agora parcialmente cobertos por espirais prateadas que parecem dançar em sua superfície. Fechos de ferro enferrujados mantêm a tampa firmemente fechada.

**Sarcophagus.** Six rusted iron latches secure the sarcophagus's lid, each inscribed with lunar runes. Opening a single latch requires a successful DC 15 Strength check. If all six latches are opened, the lid can be removed with a successful DC 15 Strength check or by the effort of any combination of characters whose combined Strength is 30 or greater.

The sarcophagus itself has been enchanted with a modified version of the [Sequester](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2618980-sequester) spell. The modification is that it doesn't render the target invisible, and the original trigger was lost to time, making the enchantment permanent. Only Vaelthas knows the method to completely dispel the effect.

**Inside, Madre Eudokaia lies imprisoned, kept in a state of prophetic stasis by the enchanted sarcophagus. If the characters look inside, read:**

> No caixão repousa uma mulher idosa em vestes cerimoniais adornadas com símbolos lunares prateados. Sua pele é pálida como o luar, e seu cabelo branco flui ao seu redor como uma auréola. Embora seus olhos estejam fechados, eles se movem rapidamente sob as pálpebras como se em sonho constante. Profecias sussurradas ocasionalmente escapam de seus lábios em uma mistura de Celestial e Abyssal.

Madre Eudokia was brought here by the Cult of Vaelthas after they found her wandering in the corrupted woods. Her prophetic abilities had been awakened and amplified through her visits to Lord Matthias Blackthorne at this very manor, where exposure to the ancient ruins and growing Aboleth influence gradually changed her. After experiencing increasingly disturbing visions, she fled into the forest one night, only to be discovered by the cultists who recognized her value. Her mind exists between planes, seeing fragments of past and future that the cult uses for their dark purposes.

**Interaction.** Madre Eudokia is under the effect of the sarcophagus's Sequester and has the *Unconscious* condition. She cannot be awakened by normal means, nor can she be targeted by Divination spells, detected by magic, or viewed remotely with magic. She remains in complete silence, breathing almost imperceptibly.

A successful DC 15 Arcana check reveals that she is under the effect of a powerful Transmutation enchantment. A successful DC 20 Arcana check reveals that it is a modified version of the *Sequester* spell emanating from the sarcophagus itself.

**Magic Circles.** The two magic circles on the floor work together to allow cultists to access Madre Eudokia's mind without awakening her. When two spellcasters position themselves in the circles (one in each) and simultaneously cast [Detect Thoughts](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619099-detect-thoughts), they can penetrate the sarcophagus's enchantment and access her prophetic visions.

A successful DC 18 Arcana check reveals that the circles are designed to work in synchrony. A successful DC 16 Investigation check observing the circles reveals that they were strategically positioned to flank the sarcophagus, and their runes are complementary.

**Latches as Trigger.** The six latches of the sarcophagus function as the lost trigger of the enchantment. When all six latches are opened simultaneously (which can be done with multiple uses of *Thaumaturgy* by the cultists), the Sequester enchantment is dispelled.

**Development.** If Madre Eudokia is awakened abruptly (by damage or forced removal), she awakens completely hostile, immediately attacking anyone in sight. Decades of forced imprisonment and manipulation have left her deeply disturbed and distrustful. She fights to the death, unable to distinguish between allies and enemies.

## Madre Eudokaia Doukas

*Medium humanoid (human), neutral*

**Armor Class** 11 (natural armor)
**Hit Points** 58 (9d8 + 18)
**Speed** 20 ft.

| Ability | Score | Modifier | Save |
|---------|--------|----------|------|
| STR | 10 | +0 | +0 |
| DEX | 8 | -1 | -1 |
| CON | 14 | +2 | +2 |
| INT | 16 | +3 | +3 |
| WIS | 18 | +4 | +6 |
| CHA | 16 | +3 | +5 |

**Saving Throws** Wis +6, Cha +5
**Skills** Arcana +6, History +6, Religion +6, Insight +7
**Damage Vulnerabilities** psychic
**Damage Resistances** necrotic
**Condition Immunities** charmed, exhaustion, frightened
**Senses** truesight 60 ft., passive Perception 14
**Languages** Common, Celestial, Abyssal
**Challenge** 3 (700 XP)

***Prophetic Fury.*** When awakened from her forced slumber, Madre Eudokia enters a state of uncontrolled rage. She has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects, and her spell attacks deal an additional 1d4 psychic damage.

***Maddened by Imprisonment.*** Madre Eudokia cannot distinguish between friend and foe. She attacks the nearest creature each turn, regardless of allegiance. She has advantage on attack rolls against creatures that awakened her from the sarcophagus.

***Spellcasting.*** Madre Eudokia is a 7th-level spellcaster. Her spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 15, +7 to hit with spell attacks). She has the following cleric spells prepared:

- Cantrips (at will): *guidance*, *light*, *thaumaturgy*
- 1st level (4 slots): *bane*, *command*, *detect evil and good*
- 2nd level (3 slots): *augury*, *hold person*, *zone of truth*
- 3rd level (3 slots): *clairvoyance*, *sending*, *speak with dead*
- 4th level (1 slot): *divination*

**Actions**

***Visionary Touch.*** *Melee Spell Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. *Hit:* 10 (3d6) psychic damage. The target must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or have the frightened condition until the end of Madre Eudokia's next turn as it experiences flashes of terrifying visions.

***Prophetic Wail (Recharge 5-6).*** Madre Eudokia lets out an otherworldly wail filled with prophetic utterances. Each creature within 30 feet that can hear her must make a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw. On a failure, a creature takes 14 (4d6) psychic damage and is stunned until the end of its next turn as its mind is flooded with overwhelming visions. On a success, a creature takes half as much damage and isn't stunned.

**Reactions**

***Glimpse the Future.*** When an attack would hit Madre Eudokia, she can use her reaction to impose disadvantage on the attack roll as she foresees the blow coming. After using this ability, Madre Eudokia must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or take 7 (2d6) psychic damage as the strain of seeing the future takes its toll.

## Moon Cultist Prophet

*Medium undead, typically neutral evil*

**Armor Class** 13 (natural armor)
**Hit Points** 38 (7d8 + 7)
**Speed** 30 ft.

| Ability | Score | Modifier | Save |
|---------|--------|----------|------|
| STR | 16 | +3 | +3 |
| DEX | 10 | +0 | +0 |
| CON | 12 | +1 | +1 |
| INT | 13 | +1 | +1 |
| WIS | 16 | +3 | +5 |
| CHA | 13 | +1 | +3 |

**Saving Throws** Wis +5, Cha +3
**Skills** Arcana +3, Deception +3, Insight +5, Religion +5
**Damage Resistances** necrotic, psychic
**Damage Immunities** poison
**Condition Immunities** exhaustion, poisoned
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 13
**Languages** Common, Abyssal, Celestial
**Challenge** 2 (450 XP)

***Undead Fortitude.*** If damage reduces the prophet to 0 hit points, it must make a Constitution saving throw with a DC of 5 + the damage taken, unless the damage is radiant or from a critical hit. On a success, the prophet drops to 1 hit point instead.

***Vision Conduit.*** While within 30 feet of Madre Eudokia, the prophet can use their action to attune to her visions. When they do, they gain advantage on their next spell attack roll or impose disadvantage on the next saving throw against one of their spells before the end of their next turn. After using this trait, the prophet takes 3 (1d6) psychic damage as the visions strain their mind.

***Spellcasting.*** The prophet is a 5th-level spellcaster. Their spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 13, +5 to hit with spell attacks). They have the following cleric spells prepared:

- Cantrips (at will): *light*, *sacred flame*, *thaumaturgy*
- 1st level (4 slots): *bane*, *command*, *guiding bolt*
- 2nd level (3 slots): *hold person*, *spiritual weapon*
- 3rd level (2 slots): *bestow curse*, *spirit guardians*

**Actions**

***Lunar Sickle.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 6 (1d6 + 3) slashing damage plus 3 (1d6) necrotic damage.

***Moonlit Glare (Recharge 5-6).*** The prophet's eyes flare with silver light. One creature within 30 feet that can see the prophet must succeed on a DC 13 Wisdom saving throw or be frightened for 1 minute. While frightened in this way, the creature is also restrained as spectral chains of moonlight bind it. The creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.
