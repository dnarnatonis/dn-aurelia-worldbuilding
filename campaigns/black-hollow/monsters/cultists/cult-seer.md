# Cult Seer

The dark priestesses of the Cult of Vaelthas, these devoted followers were drawn to Black Hollow by witnessing visions of the Whisperer in Depths or hearing whispered promises from Vaelthas himself. Cult Seers serve as the spiritual leaders of their unholy religion, typically assisted and protected by Cult Zealots. While they lack the raw physical strength of their male counterparts, they more than make up for it by using their arcane incantations to ravage the minds of those who cross their path.

With their unholy spells, they can summon forth vestiges of unnameable beings from the void to aid them in battle. They patrol the corrupted areas around Black Hollow and the Aran'keth ruins, searching for new sacrifices and conducting blasphemous rituals in service to their master's promise of immortality.

**Found in:** Black Hollow, Aran'keth ruins, leading Cult Zealots

## Stats

**AC:** 13 (Leather Armor)  
**HP:** 44 (8d8 + 8)
**Speed:** 30 ft.

**STR** 11 (+0) | **INT** 10 (+0)  
**DEX** 14 (+2) | **WIS** 14 (+2)  
**CON** 12 (+1) | **CHA** 13 (+1)

**Skills:** Deception +3, Persuasion +3, Religion +2  
**Senses:** Passive Perception 12  
**Languages:** Common

**CR**: 2 (XP 450)

## Actions

**Ritual Blade** *Melee:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 6 (1d8 + 2) slashing damage plus 7 (2d6) necrotic damage.

**Spellcasting** The seer casts one of the following spells, using Wisdom as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 12, +4 to hit with spell attacks):

At Will: *light*, *thaumaturgy*  
2/Day: *command*  
1/Day: *hold person*

## Bonus Actions

**Spiritual Weapon (2/Day)** The seer casts the *spiritual weapon* spell.
