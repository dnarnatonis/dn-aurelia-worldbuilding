# Cult Zealot

Devoted and fanatical followers of Vaelthas, these heretics were drawn to Black Hollow by visions and whispered promises of dark ascension. Cult Zealots serve as the muscle and bodyguards to the Cult Seers, using their sharpened ritual blades to inflict deep wounds while screaming blasphemous prayers to their enigmatic master. Their scarred bodies and maddened eyes speak to countless hours of self-flagellation and ritual mutilation in service to the Cult of Vaelthas.

These brawlers constantly patrol the corrupted territories around Black Hollow, seeking new sacrifices for their dark rituals or hunting down any who dare oppose Vaelthas's mission. They fight with reckless abandon, seemingly impervious to pain and motivated by unwavering belief in their master's promise of immortality.

**Found in:** Black Hollow, Aran'keth ruins, alongside Cult Seers

## Stats

**AC:** 13  
**HP:** 67 (9d8 + 27)
**Speed:** 30 ft.

**STR** 16 (+3) | **INT** 9 (-1)  
**DEX** 12 (+1) | **WIS** 11 (+0)  
**CON** 17 (+3) | **CHA** 9 (-1)

**Senses:** Passive Perception 10  
**Languages:** Common

**CR**: 2 (XP 450)

## Traits

**Bloodied Frenzy.** While bloodied, the Cult Zealot has Advantage on attack rolls and saving throws.

## Actions

**Ritual Blade** *Melee:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 9 (1d12 + 3) slashing damage.
