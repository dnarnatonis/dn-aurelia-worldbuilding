# Vaelthas (The Fallen Inquisitor)

Once Lord Thalassios Vael, High Inquisitor of the Church of Auriana, this fallen cleric has partially transcended mortality through forbidden rituals and dark pacts. Vaelthas leads the Cult of Vaelthas from the shadows of Black Hollow, promising his followers collective immortality while secretly seeking only his own eternal life. His transformation is incomplete - he has gained otherworldly power and extended life, but remains vulnerable to death.

His gaunt form bears the marks of his corruption: eyes that shift from brown to pale gray, scarred hands from ritual self-flagellation, and modified religious vestments that mock his former station. During powerful rituals, shadow-like emanations surround him and his voice carries otherworldly harmonics. He views his cult members as expendable test subjects in his quest to perfect the immortality techniques pioneered by the Hollow Lord.

**Found in:** Black Hollow, Aran'keth ruins, leading major cult rituals

## Stats

**AC:** 17 (Splint Armor)  
**HP:** 127 (15d8 + 60)
**Speed:** 30 ft.

**STR** 19 (+4) | **INT** 12 (+1)  
**DEX** 12 (+1) | **WIS** 16 (+3)  
**CON** 18 (+4) | **CHA** 14 (+2)

**Skills:** Insight +6, Perception +6, Religion +4  
**Senses:** Passive Perception 16  
**Languages:** Common

**CR**: 8 (XP 3,900)

## Actions

**Multiattack** The fallen inquisitor makes three attacks, using Corrupted Scythe or Necrotic Ray in any combination.

**Corrupted Scythe** *Melee:* +7 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. *Hit:* 9 (1d10 + 4) slashing damage plus 11 (2d10) necrotic damage, and the target can't regain Hit Points until the end of its next turn.

**Necrotic Ray** *Ranged:* +6 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. *Hit:* 22 (4d10) necrotic damage.

**Spellcasting** Vaelthas casts one of the following spells, using Wisdom as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 14):

At Will: *speak with dead*, *thaumaturgy*

## Bonus Actions

**Whispers of Despair (Recharge 5-6)** Wisdom Saving Throw: DC 14, each creature in a 20-foot emanation originating from Vaelthas. *Failure:* 14 (4d6) psychic damage, and the target has the Frightened condition until the end of its next turn. *Success:* Half damage only.
