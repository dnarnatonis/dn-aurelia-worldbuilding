# Skeleton Rabble

These skeletal remains are all that's left of the villagers and hired laborers who worked on Lord Blackthorne's ill-fated excavations beneath the manor. Now reanimated by the Hollow Lord's necromantic energies, they mindlessly serve as expendable front-line troops, their bones still bearing the tool marks and injuries from their final days of desperate digging.

Common in the Manor Ruins and occasionally seen elsewhere, these pitiful undead are often found in groups, their brittle forms acting as little more than meat shields for more dangerous foes. The tattered remnants of workmen's clothes and mining equipment still cling to their frames - a grim reminder of their mortal origins.

**Found in:** The Manor Ruins, The Mines

## Stats

**AC:** 14 (natural armor)  
**HP:** 13 (2d8 + 4)  
**Speed:** 30 ft.

**STR** 10 (+0) | **INT** 6 (-2)  
**DEX** 14 (+2) | **WIS** 8 (-1)  
**CON** 15 (+2) | **CHA** 5 (-3)

**Damage Vulnerabilities:** Bludgeoning  
**Damage Immunities:** Poison  
**Condition Immunities:** Exhaustion, Poisoned  
**Languages:** Understands languages it knew in life but can't speak

**CR**: 1/8 (XP 50)

## Actions

**Mining Pick or Shovel** *Melee Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage.
