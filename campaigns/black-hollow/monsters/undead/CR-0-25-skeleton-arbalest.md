# Skeleton Arbalest

These skeletal warriors are the reanimated remains of Lord Blackthorne's expert crossbowmen who died defending the manor during its fall. Now bound by the Hollow Lord's necromantic magic, they patrol the halls they once protected in life, their heavy crossbows and bolts a testament to their former precision.

Common in the deeper sections of the Manor Ruins, particularly near the family crypt, these undead archers retain fragments of their ranged combat training even in death. Though their movements are stiff and mechanical, they still demonstrate basic archery tactics and coordinate their attacks with other undead. Their weathered armor and well-maintained crossbows make them dangerous opponents, especially at range.

**Found in:** The Manor Ruins, The Mines

## Stats

**AC:** 15 (Chain Shirt)  
**HP:** 13 (2d8 + 4)
**Speed:** 30 ft.

**STR** 10 (+0) | **INT** 6 (-2)  
**DEX** 14 (+2) | **WIS** 8 (-1)  
**CON** 10 (+0) | **CHA** 5 (-3)
 
**Damage Vulnerabilities:** Bludgeoning  
**Damage Immunities:** Poison  
**Condition Immunities:** Exhaustion, Poisoned  
**Languages:** Understands languages it knew in life but can't speak

**CR**: 1/4 (XP 50)

## Actions

**Heavy Crossbow** *Ranged Attack:* +4 to hit, range 100/400 ft., one target. *Hit:* 7 (1d10 + 2) piercing damage.

**Dagger** *Melee Attack:* +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 4 (1d4 + 2) piercing damage.

## Bonus Actions

**Disengage.** The Skeleton Arbalest takes the Disengage action.
