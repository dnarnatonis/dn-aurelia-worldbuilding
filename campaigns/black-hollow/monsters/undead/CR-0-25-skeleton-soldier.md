# Skeleton Soldier

These skeletal warriors are the reanimated remains of Lord Blackthorne's household guards and militia who died defending the manor during its fall. Now bound by the Hollow Lord's necromantic magic, they patrol the halls they once protected in life, their rusted armor and ancient weapons a testament to their former station.

Common in the deeper sections of the Manor Ruins, particularly near the family crypt, these undead soldiers retain fragments of their military training even in death. Though their movements are stiff and mechanical, they still demonstrate basic combat tactics and coordinate their attacks with other undead. Their weathered armor and well-maintained weapons make them more formidable opponents than the scattered rabble of worker skeletons.

**Found in:** The Manor Ruins, The Mines

## Stats

**AC:** 14 (Scale Mail)  
**HP:** 13 (2d8 + 4)
**Speed:** 30 ft.

**STR** 14 (+2) | **INT** 6 (-2)  
**DEX** 10 (+0) | **WIS** 8 (-1)  
**CON** 14 (+2) | **CHA** 5 (-3)

**Damage Vulnerabilities:** Bludgeoning  
**Damage Immunities:** Poison  
**Condition Immunities:** Exhaustion, Poisoned  
**Languages:** Understands languages it knew in life but can't speak

**CR**: 1/4 (XP 50)

## Traits

**Pack Tactics.** The Skeleton Soldier has Advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the Skeleton Soldier's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally doesn't have the Incapacitated condition.

## Actions

**Spear** *Melee:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. *Hit:* 6 (1d8 + 2) piercing damage.

**Throw Spear** *Ranged Attack:* +2 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. *Hit:* 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage.
