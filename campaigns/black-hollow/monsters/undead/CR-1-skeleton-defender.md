# Skeleton Defender

Once a proud shieldbearer, the Bone Defenders were honorable and loyal soldiers that served Lord Blackthorne's family in ages long since passed. Holding the line on the battlefield with shield and trusty morningstar, those who were slain on the field of battle were given a place within the crypts of the Ruins among those who fought and died on the battlefield for the family's name and glory. However, when their bodies died, so too did their loyalties...

When the Hollow Lord's necromantic magic desecrated his grave, it brought this poor soldier back as a dark creature to protect his fellow undead within the Ruins. Like an undead mockery of a stalwart guardian, it protects its unholy allies with its own body, holding the line with its sturdy shield and armor while those that hide behind it attack with impunity. These skeletal remains are lumbering with their heavy armor, but their unwavering determination makes them formidable protectors.

Bone Defenders are enemies commonly encountered within the deeper sections of the Manor Ruins. Their large shield leaves them well-armored and capable of taking a beating, and they use this resilience to protect the other undead soldiers guarding the area. The Defender always appears at the front and aims to frustrate attackers with its hefty protection and ability to intercept attacks meant for allies.

**Found in:** The Manor Ruins, The Mines

## Stats

**AC:** 17 (Chain Mail + Shield)  
**HP:** 21 (6d6)
**Speed:** 25 ft.

**STR** 15 (+2) | **INT** 6 (-2)  
**DEX** 10 (+0) | **WIS** 8 (-1)  
**CON** 15 (+2) | **CHA** 5 (-3)

**Damage Vulnerabilities:** Bludgeoning  
**Damage Immunities:** Poison  
**Condition Immunities:** Exhaustion, Poisoned  
**Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 9  
**Languages:** Understands languages it knew in life but can't speak

**CR**: 1 (XP 200)

## Actions

**Multiattack.** The Skeleton Defender makes two Morningstar attacks.

**Morningstar.** *Melee Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 6 (1d8 + 2) bludgeoning damage.
**Shield Bash.** *Melee Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 4 (1d4 + 2) bludgeoning damage. If the target is a Medium or smaller creature, it must succeed on a DC 12 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.

## Bonus Actions

**Defensive Stance.** The Skeleton Defender gains a +2 bonus to AC until the start of its next turn.

## Reactions

**Protective Swap.** When a creature the Skeleton Defender can see within 5 feet of it is targeted by an attack, the Skeleton Defender can use its reaction to swap places with that creature. The Skeleton Defender becomes the target of the attack instead.
