# Skeleton Captain

So vast were Lord Blackthorne's ancient armies that they needed loyal men to lead those armies. These Captains, Commanders and Generals fought many campaigns against any foreign invaders that dared to lay claim onto the land of their lord. So great and terrible was their strength that they made the ground tremble and left their enemies scattered, stunned and crushed before them. For their undying fidelity they were given an honor grave within the crypts of the Ruins among those who fought and died on the battlefield for the family's name and glory. However, no matter how undying their fidelity was to the Blackthorne family, it did not stop the Hollow Lord from turning them into undying abominations.

Risen from their Grave as a hulking Goliath of bone clad in thick metal plate armor, the Bone Captain is a fearsome and terrible foe, capable of devastating even the most capable warriors with the sheer bone-crushing brutality of its massive greatsword. Being a commanding presence amongst the Ruins, he leads the undying armies of undead for his new dark master or patrols the decaying and crumbling halls of the Ruins accompanied by either fellow Undead or cultists. Sometimes he will be summoned by the Hollow Lord to aid him in battle, which may often prove fatal for any would-be group of heroes.

The Bone Captain is an enemy that appears in Veteran-tier or harder expeditions in the Ruins. These undead soldiers, befitting their rank, are tough and strong, using their massive greatswords to devastate their enemies. The weight of their strikes can cause the very ground to quake, leaving those affected dazed and reeling.

The Captain always appears in front line of its party with ranged/support undead and/or cultists behind him. Its main ability is Ground Pound, a mighty smash that can stun an entire party. Sometimes it can perform a single, bone-crushing strike aimed at one of the front-line opponents, dealing massive damage and making it more likely that they will be stunned.

**Found in:** The Manor Ruins, The Mines

## Stats

**AC:** 17 (Splint Armor)  
**HP:** 65 (10d8 + 20)
**Speed:** 30 ft.

**STR** 16 (+3) | **INT** 6 (-2)  
**DEX** 13 (+1) | **WIS** 8 (-1)  
**CON** 14 (+2) | **CHA** 5 (-3)

**Skills:** Athletics +5, Perception +2
**Damage Vulnerabilities:** Bludgeoning
**Damage Immunities:** Poison  
**Condition Immunities:** Exhaustion, Poisoned  
**Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 9  
**Languages:** Understands languages it knew in life but can't speak

**CR**: 3 (XP 700)

## Actions

**Multiattack.** The Skeleton Captain makes two Greatsword.

**Greatsword.** *Melee Attack Roll:* +5, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 10 (2d6 + 3) Slashing damage.
## Reactions

**Parry.** When the Skeleton Captain is hit by a melee attack roll while holding a weapon, it can use its reaction to add 2 to its AC against that attack, potentially causing it to miss.
