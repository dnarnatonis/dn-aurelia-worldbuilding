# The Hollow Lord

Lord Blackthorne's pursuit of forbidden knowledge began with humble experiments in mastery over life and death. His ambition grew limitless, and he invited several scholars to study with him, only to murder them in their sleep after learning all their secrets. To demonstrate his newfound powers, he brought them back through necromancy with much of their intellect intact, but with none of their humanity left.

Drunk on success, Blackthorne attempted to apply his perfected necromantic methods to himself, believing he could become an immortal undead lord while retaining his intelligence. The ritual went catastrophically wrong, transforming him into the mindless abomination now known as the Hollow Lord. He stripped away what remained of his former human self and became a creature of pure evil; towering, fierce, terrible, nightmare made material.

The Hollow Lord has perverted the natural cycles of life, death, decay and renewal within the Manor Ruins, replacing them with his own twisted version - an endless cycle of desecration and reanimation of the dead, where life is death and the dead are alive. By the time adventurers arrive at the Estate, the Manor Ruins are completely overrun by the Undead under his command.

While this devil has unholy hatred for all that lives, he seems to have an unusual tolerance towards the Cultists who have taken over parts of the Manor Ruins and turned them into temples for the Old Gods. Whether this is through some dark pact or because his necromantic arts and the Cultists' blasphemous religion have more in common than anyone knows remains a mystery.

The only way to end this cycle of death and unlife is to slay the evil puppet master behind this horde of undying abominations, so that the dead can stay dead.

## Appearance

The Hollow Lord is a towering, nightmarish figure draped in the remnants of his former nobility. His once-fine robes have been transformed into flowing crimson and black vestments that seem to writhe with their own dark life. The fabric appears to be woven from shadow and dried blood, billowing around his gaunt frame like funeral shrouds.

Where Lord Blackthorne's face once was, there is now only a void beneath a deep hood - a hollow space filled with shifting darkness and occasional flickers of malevolent red light. Skeletal hands emerge from the sleeves of his robes, their bone-white fingers adorned with tarnished rings bearing arcane symbols that seem to shift and writhe when observed. These hands constantly clutch at ancient scrolls and necromantic texts, the parchment crumbling at his touch yet somehow remaining intact.

His presence seems to warp the very air around him, creating an aura of decay and despair. The ground beneath his feet withers and cracks, and the temperature drops noticeably in his vicinity. When he moves, it is with an unnatural, gliding motion that suggests he no longer truly walks but rather drifts through the physical world like a living shadow.

## Behavior

The Hollow Lord is an Unholy/Eldritch master that lurks in the deepest chambers of the Manor Ruins. Once the noble Lord Blackthorne, he is now a mindless abomination driven purely by necromantic instinct, with little to no humanity remaining. He uses his corrupted mastery of death magic to bring the corpses buried throughout his ancestral home back to life as his servants.

The Hollow Lord serves as the puppet master behind the endless hordes of skeletal warriors that patrol the Manor Ruins. From his position deep within the family crypts, he commands legions of undead through his dark will, continuously raising fallen warriors to serve his twisted purposes. His presence corrupts the very foundations of the manor, turning what was once a place of rest for the honored dead into a nightmare realm where death holds no meaning.

The most terrifying aspect of the Hollow Lord is his ability to instantly replenish his fallen minions. Each time his skeletal servants fall in battle, he can immediately raise new ones from the countless corpses buried throughout the Manor Ruins. This creates an endless cycle of death and resurrection, wearing down even the most determined adventurers through sheer attrition.

**Found in:** The Manor Ruins (Deepest Crypts)

## Actions

_**Regeneration.**_ The Hollow Lord regains 10 Hit Points at the start of each of its turns. If the Hollow Lord takes Fire or Radiant damage, this trait doesn’t function at the start of its next turn. Its body is destroyed only if it starts its turn with 0 Hit Points and doesn’t regenerate.

**Multiattack.** The Hollow Lord makes three **Necrotic Burst attacks.

**Necrotic Burst.** *Melee or Ranged Attack:* Roll +6, reach 5 ft. or range 120 ft., 1d8 + 3 Necrotic damage.

**Spellcasting.** The Hollow Lord casts one of the following spells, using Intelligence as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 14):

- **At Will:** Detect Magic, Light, Mage Armor (included in AC), Mage Hand, Prestidigitation
- **2/Day Each:** [Erupting Earth](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2380-erupting-earth) (level 4 version), [Fear](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2618872-fear)
- **1/Day:** [Contagion](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619063-contagion)

## Bonus Actions

**Misty Step (3/Day).** The Hollow Lord casts Misty Step using the same spellcasting ability as Spellcasting.

## Reactions

**Protective Magic (3/Day).** The Hollow Lord casts [Counterspell](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619072-counterspell) or [Shield](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619019-shield) in response to the spell's trigger, using the same spellcasting ability as Spellcasting.
