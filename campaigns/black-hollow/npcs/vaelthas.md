# Vaelthas (The Fallen Inquisitor)

---

## **Classificação NPC**

**Tipo:** Plotter  
**Função:** Primary Antagonist / False Prophet  
**Importância:** Major Nemesis

---

## **Informações Básicas**

### **Nome**

**Vaelthas** (formerly Lord Thalassios Vael, High Inquisitor of the Church of Auriana)

### **Trabalho/Função**

Self-proclaimed prophet and leader of the Cult of Vaelthas. Secretly collaborates with the Gravedigger to achieve personal immortality while deceiving his followers into believing he will grant them eternal life.

### **Objetivo**

Achieve true immortality for himself through perfecting the Hollow Lord's experiments, while using his cult as unwitting test subjects and tools for his research.

### **Uso na Narrativa**

- **As Primary Antagonist:** The ultimate enemy for Nikephoros, representing corrupted faith and forbidden knowledge
- **As False Prophet:** Leads a cult that believes in collective immortality while seeking only personal transcendence
- **As Research Partner:** Collaborates with the Gravedigger to refine necromantic techniques
- **As Catalyst:** His past actions with Anastasía set the entire tragedy in motion

---

## **Background: The Fall from Grace**

### **The High Inquisitor**

Lord Thalassios Vael was once among the most respected High Inquisitors in the Church of Auriana, known for his zealous pursuit of heretics and his deep theological knowledge. His position granted him access to the Church's most forbidden archives, where he first encountered references to primordial entities that preceded the current gods.

### **The Corruption**

What began as scholarly curiosity evolved into obsession. Vael began conducting secret experiments, using his authority to:

- Access banned theological texts and arcane knowledge
- Perform forbidden rituals under the guise of "purification ceremonies"
- Sacrifice devoted believers in hidden chambers beneath the cathedral
- Study the boundaries between life, death, and undeath

### **The Discovery**

When templars Nikephoros Komnenos and Anastasía Lysandros uncovered his activities, Vael was forced to accelerate his plans. In the Cathedral of White Fire, he performed his most ambitious ritual - sacrificing Anastasía before Nikephoros's eyes while binding her soul to create a permanent anchor between the planes.

### **The Transformation**

This "sublime sacrifice" allowed Vael to partially transcend his mortal form, gaining otherworldly knowledge and power. However, it was incomplete - he achieved enhanced abilities but not true immortality. After fleeing to Black Hollow, he completely assumed the identity of Vaelthas and began building his cult.

## **Current State**

### **Physical Description**

**Human Form (Most Common):**

- Tall, gaunt figure with prematurely aged features
- Eyes that shift between brown and an unsettling pale gray
- Always wears modified religious vestments, corrupted versions of Church of Auriana regalia
- Scarred hands from ritual self-flagellation
- Voice carries unnatural resonance, especially during sermons

**Partially Transformed State (During Rituals):**

- Shadowlike emanations surround his form
- Eyes become entirely pale, pupilless
- Voice becomes layered with otherworldly whispers
- Temporary manifestation of spectral wings or tendrils

### **Mental State**

- **Obsessed with Immortality:** Everything he does serves his ultimate goal of achieving true eternal life
- **Manipulative Genius:** Uses religious fervor and promises of immortality to control his followers
- **Paranoid:** Constantly watches for signs of betrayal, especially from the Gravedigger
- **Consumed by Hatred:** His feelings toward Nikephoros have evolved beyond simple revenge into cosmic obsession

### **Current Abilities**

- **Necromantic Mastery:** Extensive knowledge of undeath and soul manipulation
- **Planar Insight:** Can perceive and partially access other planes of existence
- **Divine Corruption:** Perverted versions of clerical abilities, powered by dark entities
- **Enhanced Longevity:** His partial transformation has extended his lifespan significantly
- **Cult Leadership:** Charismatic speaker who can inspire fanatical devotion

## **Motivação Central**

### **Primary Desire: Personal Immortality**

Vaelthas desperately seeks to complete his transformation and achieve true immortality. Unlike his followers, who believe the entire cult will be granted eternal life, Vaelthas sees them as expendable resources in his research.

### **The Hollow Lord Connection**

Working secretly with the Gravedigger, Vaelthas studies Lord Matthias Blackthorne's failed experiments to understand:

- What went wrong with Blackthorne's immortality ritual
- How to avoid the mindless state that resulted
- Methods to perfect the soul-binding process
- Ways to retain intelligence and personality through undeath

### **The False Promise**

His cult believes that through devotion and sacrifice, all members will achieve collective immortality in a "Great Awakening." In reality, Vaelthas plans to:

- Use cult members as test subjects for perfected immortality techniques
- Harvest their souls and life force to fuel his own transformation
- Create an army of intelligent undead servants under his command
- Establish himself as an immortal god-king ruling over the undead

---

## **Resources**

### **What He Controls**

- **The Cult of Vaelthas:** Hundreds of devoted followers across multiple cells
- **Hidden Sanctuaries:** Secret worship sites throughout Black Hollow and beyond
- **Forbidden Knowledge:** Extensive library of heretical texts and necromantic rituals
- **Undead Network:** Growing collection of intelligent undead servants
- **Ancient Artifacts:** Relics gathered through cult activities and excavations

### **What He Seeks**

- **The Perfect Immortality Formula:** Completion of Blackthorne's research
- **More Test Subjects:** Both willing cult members and unwilling victims
- **Ancient Power Sources:** Access to convergence points and planar nexuses
- **Elimination of Threats:** Destruction of Nikephoros and anyone who might expose him

### **His Limitations**

- **Incomplete Transformation:** Still partially mortal and vulnerable
- **Dependence on the Gravedigger:** Needs her expertise and resources
- **Cult Management:** Must maintain the illusion of collective salvation
- **Church Pursuit:** Still hunted by imperial authorities and Church inquisitors

---

## **Relationship with the Gravedigger**

### **Surface Partnership**

Vaelthas believes he and the Gravedigger are equal partners working toward similar goals. He provides:

- Cult members as test subjects and workers
- Access to forbidden theological knowledge
- Religious cover for necromantic activities
- Networks of followers throughout the region

### **Hidden Manipulation**

Unknown to Vaelthas, the Gravedigger considers him a useful tool rather than a true partner. She:

- Subtly guides his research toward her own objectives
- Uses his cult network for Cabal intelligence gathering
- Plans to eventually eliminate him once his usefulness ends
- Keeps the most valuable discoveries hidden from him

### **Mutual Deception**

Both parties hide their true intentions:

- **Vaelthas** secretly plans to betray the Gravedigger once he achieves immortality
- **The Gravedigger** views him as a disposable asset in her larger schemes
- Neither fully trusts the other, but both benefit from the collaboration
- Their partnership is held together by mutual necessity rather than loyalty

---

## **Connection to the Player Characters**

### **The Nikephoros Obsession**

Vaelthas's hatred for Nikephoros transcends simple revenge. He views the paladin as:

- **The Perfect Test Subject:** Nikephoros's connection to Anastasía's spirit makes him invaluable for soul-binding research
- **Personal Nemesis:** The one person who truly understands his fall from grace
- **Cosmic Threat:** Someone whose faith and determination could potentially undo his plans
- **Final Trophy:** Corrupting or destroying Nikephoros would validate his transformation

### **The Anastasía Factor**

The spirit of Anastasía bound to Nikephoros represents:

- **Unfinished Business:** Her soul's unique state offers insights into perfected undeath
- **Research Opportunity:** Studying their connection could improve his immortality techniques
- **Emotional Weapon:** Using her against Nikephoros provides psychological advantage
- **Power Source:** Her spiritual energy could fuel his final transformation

## **Tactics and Methods**

### **Cult Operations**

- **Recruitment:** Targets desperate, grieving, or ambitious individuals with promises of eternal life
- **Indoctrination:** Gradual exposure to increasingly extreme rituals and beliefs
- **Cell Structure:** Maintains separation between groups to prevent total exposure if discovered
- **Cover Stories:** Uses legitimate religious activities to mask forbidden practices

### **Research Methods**

- **Human Experimentation:** Tests immortality techniques on volunteer and captive subjects
- **Ritual Sacrifice:** Harvests souls and life force through carefully orchestrated ceremonies
- **Collaborative Study:** Works with the Gravedigger to analyze Blackthorne's failures
- **Archaeological Excavation:** Searches for ancient artifacts and power sources

### **Dealing with Threats**

- **Corruption:** Attempts to turn enemies into allies through promises or coercion
- **Elimination:** Uses cult members and undead servants to remove persistent threats
- **Misdirection:** Creates false leads and cover stories to protect his true activities
- **Strategic Retreat:** Abandons positions when necessary to preserve his larger plans

---

## **Weaknesses**

### **Psychological Vulnerabilities**

- **Obsession with Immortality:** Single-minded focus creates blind spots and poor decisions
- **Pride:** His transformation has made him arrogant and prone to underestimating enemies
- **Paranoia:** Constant fear of betrayal makes him waste resources on internal security
- **Past Guilt:** Deep down, still struggles with the magnitude of his fall from grace

### **Practical Limitations**

- **Incomplete Transformation:** Still vulnerable to mortality and death
- **Cult Dependencies:** Relies on followers who could potentially turn against him
- **Research Requirements:** Needs specific materials and conditions for his experiments
- **Hidden Enemies:** The Church, imperial authorities, and even some cult members seek his destruction

### **Relationship Weaknesses**

- **The Gravedigger:** Unaware of her true loyalties and plans
- **Cult Loyalty:** Many followers would abandon him if they learned his true intentions
- **Nikephoros Connection:** His obsession with the paladin clouds his judgment
- **Isolation:** Has no true friends or allies, only tools and enemies

- **Cold Pragmatist:** Utterly ruthless when pursuing his goals
- **Intellectual Snob:** Looks down on those he considers intellectually inferior
- **Paranoid Leader:** Constantly evaluates others for signs of betrayal or usefulness

- **To Cult Members:** Uses archaic religious language mixed with promises of eternal glory
- **To Collaborators:** Speaks in clinical, academic terms about research and methods
- **To Enemies:** Combines theological condemnation with personal venom
- **Internal Monologue:** Constantly calculates costs, benefits, and risks of every action

### **Mannerisms**

- **Ritual Gestures:** Often performs small religious gestures, corrupted versions of Church practices
- **Eye Contact:** Intense, unsettling stare that seems to look through people
- **Voice Modulation:** His voice takes on otherworldly harmonics during emotional moments
- **Hand Movements:** Unconsciously traces necromantic symbols when thinking

## **Secrets and Hidden Information**

### **Known Only to Vaelthas**

- His research has identified the specific flaw in Blackthorne's immortality ritual
- He has successfully created several intelligent undead servants
- His transformation is slowly corrupting his original personality
- He maintains secret correspondence with other corrupted Church officials

### **Known Only to the Gravedigger**

- Vaelthas's partial transformation is actually failing and he is slowly dying
- His research could be more valuable than the man himself
- She has contingency plans to eliminate him if necessary
- His cult network provides excellent cover for Cabal operations

### **Unknown to Anyone**

- Anastasía's bound spirit may hold the key to reversing all of his transformations
- His original faith in Auriana still exists, buried deep in his corrupted soul
- The primordial entities he contacted have their own plans for him
- His pursuit of immortality may actually be accelerating his death
