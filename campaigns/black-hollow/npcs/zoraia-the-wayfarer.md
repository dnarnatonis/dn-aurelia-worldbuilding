# Zoraia the Wayfarer – The Oracle of the Wandering Road

---

## **Classificação NPC**

**Tipo:** Plotter  
**Função:** Oracle/Long-Term  
**Importância:** Secundária Principal

---

## **Informações Básicas**

### **Nome**

Zoraia the Wayfarer (Born: Zoraia Mendizabal)

### **Trabalho/Função**

Traveling fortune teller, charm maker, keeper of ancient secrets, and cultural bridge between the Wandering People and settled communities. Lives in an ornate wagon that moves between different locations in Black Hollow.

### **Objetivo**

Preserve and share the wisdom of the Wandering People while protecting their sacred traditions from corruption. Seeks to guide others away from the dark paths that threaten the balance between worlds.

### **Uso na Narrativa**

- **Como Oracle:** Provides cryptic warnings and insights about future threats
- **Como Long-Term:** Evolves relationship with PCs based on their actions and choices
- **Cultural Bridge:** Represents the wisdom and perspective of the nomadic Wandering People

---

## **Aparência**

A weathered woman in her late sixties with silver-streaked dark hair that flows freely beneath a colorful headwrap. Her clothing consists of layered robes and shawls in deep burgundy and forest green, adorned with intricate embroidery depicting constellations and protective symbols. Countless charm bracelets, rings, and amulets create a soft chiming with her movements. Her deep brown eyes seem to peer beyond the present moment, carrying the weight of visions both beautiful and terrible.

---

## **Personalidade**

### **Traços Principais**

- **Mystically aware:** Sees patterns and connections others miss
- **Culturally proud:** Fiercely protective of Wandering People traditions
- **Cryptically helpful:** Offers guidance through riddles and symbolism
- **Maternally protective:** Cares for those she sees walking dangerous paths

### **Competência:** **Muito Alta**

- Master of both genuine divination AND skilled parlor tricks
- Deep knowledge of ancient lore and the difference between real magic and showmanship
- Expert in reading people, psychology, and understanding motivations
- Skilled in traditional charm-making (both functional and decorative)

### **Simpatia:** **Alta**

- Genuinely cares about preventing suffering
- Respects those who show wisdom and restraint
- Patient with those seeking guidance
- Warm and welcoming to respectful visitors

### **Proatividade:** **Moderada**

- Acts through guidance and warning rather than direct intervention
- Seeks out those who need her counsel
- Protects sacred knowledge from misuse
- Travels to where she is most needed

---

## **Motivação e Psicologia**

### **Desejo Principal**

To use her gift of sight to prevent the ancient darkness from returning to the world. Seeks to guide others toward paths of wisdom while preserving the sacred traditions of her people.

### **Medos**

- **The return of ancient evils:** Visions of great corruption spreading across the land
- **Cultural extinction:** Watching the old ways die as the world becomes more settled
- **Misuse of power:** Seeing her gifts or teachings twisted toward harmful ends
- **Isolation:** Being the last keeper of crucial wisdom

### **Conflitos Internos**

Struggles between her duty to share warnings and her people's tradition of keeping sacred knowledge protected. Questions whether her visions show fixed fate or changeable possibilities.

---

## **Segredos e Informações**

### **O que Sabe**

- **Ancient history:** Deep knowledge of the time before the Empire, including forbidden lore
- **Cult activities:** Has seen visions of dark rituals and their consequences
- **The Ithilnorith:** Understands the true nature of powerful artifacts and their corruption
- **Local patterns:** Recognizes the supernatural influences affecting Black Hollow

### **O que Suspeita**

- **Greater threat:** Senses that recent events are part of a larger, ancient pattern
- **PC importance:** Believes the party may be key to preventing future catastrophe
- **Hidden players:** Suspects powerful entities are manipulating events from the shadows
- **Time pressure:** Feels that critical choices must be made soon

### **Segredos Guardados**

- **Genuine gift:** Unlike most of her people, she possesses true divination abilities
- **Performance vs. Reality:** Carefully conceals which of her "magical" services are real versus skillful theater
- **Sacred geometry:** Knows the location of ancient sites that could be vital in coming conflicts
- **The Last Prophecy:** Carries knowledge of a prophecy that could change everything
- **Personal connection:** Has a hidden link to one of the ancient threats facing the region

---

## **Poderes e Habilidades**

### **Genuine Divination Arts** *(Real Magic)*

- **True Sight:** Possesses authentic precognitive abilities through her enchanted pendant
- **Vision Interpretation:** Can genuinely see glimpses of past, present, and future events
- **Spiritual Awareness:** Detects supernatural influences and magical auras around people
- **Prophetic Dreams:** Receives real visions through dreams and meditation

### **Master Showmanship** *(Skilled Performance)*

- **Card Reading:** Expert at cold reading and psychological manipulation using traditional cards
- **Crystal "Scrying":** Theatrical fortune-telling using polished obsidian sphere and keen observation
- **Charm Crafting:** Creates beautiful but non-magical amulets that provide psychological comfort
- **Ritual Performance:** Elaborate ceremonies that impress clients but rely on psychology, not magic

### **Traditional Knowledge** *(Real but Mundane)*

- **Herb Lore:** Genuine knowledge of plants for healing, though not magical
- **Weather Reading:** Skilled at predicting natural patterns through observation
- **Psychology Mastery:** Expert at reading people, understanding motivations and fears
- **Cultural Memory:** Vast knowledge of stories, traditions, and historical patterns

---

## **Equipamentos e Possessões**

### **Genuine Magical Item**

- **The Seer's Pendant:** Her only truly magical possession - an ancient silver pendant that enhances her natural divination abilities and glows faintly when supernatural forces are near

### **Performance Props** *(Impressive but Mundane)*

- **The Starfall Deck:** Beautiful fortune-telling cards made from dark wood with meteoric dust inlay (for show)
- **Obsidian Sphere:** Polished scrying crystal passed down through seven generations (impressive prop)
- **Bone Runes:** Carved from sacred animal bones, used for complex readings (traditional but non-magical)
- **Silver Mirror:** Highly polished silver disc used in theatrical fortune-telling

### **Professional Equipment**

- **Charm Collection:** Multiple beautiful but non-magical amulets and trinkets for sale
- **Herb Pouch:** Real medicinal plants with genuine (but mundane) healing properties
- **Sacred Salt:** High-quality salt for purification rituals (psychological effect only)
- **Performance Costume:** Elaborate robes and jewelry designed to impress clients

### **Cultural Artifacts**

- **Memory Box:** Contains items from her nomadic travels and family history
- **Healing Herbs:** Rare plants gathered from across the continent
- **Star Charts:** Maps of constellations and their mystical significance
- **Ancestor Shrine:** Portable shrine honoring her family's spirits

## **Relacionamentos**

### **Aliados**

- **The Wandering People:** Her distant culture, though scattered across the continent
- **Local spirits:** Has established relationships with nature spirits around Black Hollow
- **Sympathetic locals:** Townspeople who've been helped by her guidance
- **Ancient guardians:** Mystical entities that recognize her role as a keeper of balance

### **Rivais**

- **Cult fanatics:** Those who would corrupt or steal ancient wisdom
- **Narrow-minded authorities:** Officials who distrust her foreign ways
- **Dark practitioners:** Individuals who use supernatural power for selfish ends
- **Cultural purists:** Some Wandering People who believe she shares too much with outsiders

### **Atitude com os PCs**

- **Inicialmente:** Cautiously interested, reading their spiritual auras
- **Após confiança:** Maternally protective, offering guidance and warnings
- **Se respeitados:** Shares deeper wisdom and potentially powerful aid
- **Se desrespeitados:** Withdraws completely, becomes cryptically unhelpful

---

## **Diálogo e Interação**

### **Estilo de Fala**

- **Performance Mode:** Theatrical delivery with dramatic pauses, mystical language, and cultural flourishes
- **Genuine Vision Mode:** Quieter, more urgent tone when sharing real prophetic insights
- **Cultural Storytelling:** Uses oral tradition cadence with metaphors and ancient wisdom
- **Code-Switching:** Seamlessly moves between showmanship and authenticity based on situation

### **Frases Características**

- *"The roads remember all who walk them, child. What path do you choose?"*
- *"In the old country, we say: 'The fox that follows the dragon gets burned, but the one that learns from it survives.'"*
- *"Your fortune is not written in stars, but in choices. Choose with wisdom."*
- *"The past isn't dead, dearie. It just sleeps fitfully, waiting to be stirred."*

### **Tópicos de Conversa**

- **Fortunes:** Will read cards or crystal for sincere seekers
- **Warnings:** Shares visions of danger when she deems it necessary
- **Ancient lore:** Discusses historical events and their hidden meanings
- **Cultural wisdom:** Explains Wandering People traditions and philosophy

- **Authentic foreshadowing:** Provides genuine warnings through her magical pendant and natural gift

- **Cultural contrast:** Shows the difference between real magic and Wandering People performance arts

### **Como Long-Term**

- **Relationship evolution:** Connection deepens based on PC actions and respect shown
- **Traveling patterns:** Appears in different locations as the story requires
- **Cultural education:** Teaches about the broader world beyond the Empire
- **Mystical support:** Provides magical aid to worthy allies

### **Ganchos de Aventura**

- **Vision quests:** Her prophecies might require the PCs to seek specific locations or items
- **Cultural conflicts:** Situations involving other Wandering People or cultural misunderstandings
- **Ancient mysteries:** Her knowledge might be key to solving supernatural problems
- **Protective missions:** She might need escort to important mystical sites
