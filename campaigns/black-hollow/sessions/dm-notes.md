## The Ithilnorith (The Thirsting Codex)

### Current State: "Sealed Chronicle"

- Currently in its "Sealed Chronicle" state
- Appears as ancient High Elven spellbook
- True nature sealed by last uncorrupted Ithilthar'ai

### Observable Properties

- Lunar cipher only readable in moonlight
- Appears as eccentric wizard's spellbook
- Contains hints of deeper knowledge
- Cold to touch, makes animals uneasy

### Zoraia's Vision

- Foresees potential for both salvation and corruption
- Timing of warning may be crucial for future developments

### Connection to Plot

- Primary objective of the Prophet's Crypt operation
- Located using the Prophet's gift
- May contain knowledge about immortality
- Could hold information about the Aboleth's prison
- Loss represents first major setback to [[the-gravedigger|Gravedigger's]] plans

## The Fallen Manor - Level 1 Developments

### Faction Dynamics

- **Kobolds**: Willing to negotiate, focused on dragon recovery
- **Goblins**: Initially hostile, but open to dialogue
- **[[Nyxis]]**: Young dragon seeking adventure
- **Hidden cultist influence** through "gifts" to kobolds

### Key Items

**[[Lumenaris]]** (+1 Longsword)
- Ancient Dragon Order artifact
- Responds to corruption's presence
- Self-selecting: becomes heavy and cold in unworthy hands
- Significant connection to anti-corruption mandate

**Ancient Texts**
- "The Aureate Codex" - Religious treatise on Auriana and her divine family ([[The Knight]])
- "The City of the Gods" - Historical chronicle ([[The Sage]])
- "Sacred Architecture and Divine Combat Arts" - Archaeological study of martial traditions ([[The Monk]])

**Other Items**
- Sending stone from [[silas-the-raven|Silas]]
- Southern door key (kobold possession, now lost)

### Strategic Implications

- Cave-in may be blessing in disguise, preventing three-way conflict
- [[Nyxis]]'s departure could destabilize local power structure
- [[silas-the-raven|Silas's]] continued influence through dead drops
- Cultist manipulation of kobolds suggests ongoing surveillance

### Potential Developments

- Goblin reaction to [[Nyxis]]'s departure
- Kobold response if dragon chooses not to return
- Cultist response to changing power dynamics
- Deeper level threats may be drawn to surface chaos
