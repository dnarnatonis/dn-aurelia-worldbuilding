# Session 1: The Call of Black Hollow

**Date:** 35th-37th day of Skiabloom (Skianthemon), 3692 AB  
**Previous:** N/A | **Next:** [[session-02-prophets-crypt]]

---

## Summary

The party arrives in [[Black Hollow]] after surviving an ambush by the [[bandit-lords-black-sword-company|Black Sword Company]]. They meet the major factions and begin to understand the threats facing the town.

---

## Prologue: The Last Coach to Black Hollow

**Location:** Duskport Coach Station  
**Date:** 35th day of Skiabloom (Skianthemon), 3692 AB (20th year of Celestara II's reign)  
**Season:** Late Autumn (Kyklos Hesperis)  
**Lunar Phase:** Photomera (Day of Light) of the 5th week (Waning Moon)  
**Weather:** Overcast, dropping temperature, cold northern winds

### Key Events

- Party assembled at Duskport Coach Station
- Met **Raul Luthen** (experienced Gilded Dawn guard) and **Mari "Swift"** (frontier scout)
- Observed ominous signs: empty station, wary travelers, watching ravens
- Received first hints of danger from Mari's map study of Withered Thicket

### Notable Quotes

> "You're not the first they've sent. Hope you're smarter than the last ones."  
> — Raul Luthen

> "The road has changed since my last passing. The Withered Thicket... it grows."  
> — Mari "Swift"

## The Withered Thicket Ambush

**Date:** 37th day of Skiabloom (Skianthemon), 3692 AB  
**Location:** Withered Thicket, near Black Hollow  
**Lunar Phase:** Selenemera (Day of the Moon) of the 5th week (Waning Moon)  
**Conditions:** Dense fog, late afternoon

### Combat Encounter

- Ambush carefully planned at a sharp bend
- [[bandit-lords-black-sword-company|Black Sword Company]] mercenaries identified
- Ancient tree blocking the road
- Carriage slipped in mud when Raul pulled the reins
- Professional military tactics employed

### Outcome

- ✅ All party members survived
- ✅ Mari and Raul remained active throughout combat
- ✅ Common mercenaries eliminated
- ⚠️ **Black Sword Company leader escaped** - connection to larger plot

## Arrival at Black Hollow

**Date:** 37th day of Skiabloom (Skianthemon), 3692 AB  
**Time:** Dusk  
**Conditions:** Dispersing fog, unnatural silence

### First Impressions

- Met [[aldric-thornfeld|Aldric Thornfeld]], town Keeper, at north gate
- Cordial but cautious reception
- Aldric's comment suggesting "someone knew you were coming"
- Escorted to the Hollow Hearth tavern

## The Hollow Hearth Gathering

### Key NPCs Encountered

- [[aldric-thornfeld|Aldric Thornfeld]] - Town Keeper, local authority figure
- **Gareth** - Tavern owner, former soldier
- [[marla-magpie|Marla]] - [[the-veil|The Veil]] representative, offered trade deal
- [[liriel-thornshade|Liriel]] - Elven ranger, knows safe paths and local threats
- [[dean-theodosios|Dean Theodosios]] - Local religious leader, concerned about regional corruption

### Diplomatic Developments

**Primary Alliances:**

- Aligned with [[aldric-thornfeld|Aldric Thornfeld]] as legitimate authority
- Partnership with [[liriel-thornshade|Liriel]] for terrain knowledge
- Cooperation with **Mari "Swift"** of the Rangers

**The Veil Agreement:**

- Negotiated with [[marla-magpie|Marla]] for stolen supplies recovery
- 50/50 split on found treasure
- One week of free supplies from [[the-veil|The Veil]]
- Specific details pending

### Intelligence Gathered

**The Crypt:**

- Located in local cemetery
- Potential source of supernatural disturbances
- Contains important regional secrets

**Faction Analysis:**

- [[the-veil|The Veil]] - Controls black market, offers resources
- **Church** - Diminished influence but retains supernatural knowledge
- **Rangers** - Strong terrain knowledge, limited resources
