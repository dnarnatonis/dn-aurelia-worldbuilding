## Summary

The party investigates the cemetery crypt, encounters cultists performing a dark ritual, and recovers the mysterious [[Ithilnorith]]. Political tensions between [[aldric-thornfeld|Aldric]] and [[dean-theodosios|Dean Theodosios]] begin to surface over how to handle a captured cultist.
