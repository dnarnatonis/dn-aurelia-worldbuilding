### Recovery of Lumenaris

**[[Lumenaris]]** (+1 Longsword, "The One Who Watches in the Light")
- Ancient blade blessed by Aelyndria and the Metallic Dragons
- Pale steel blade with draconic runes that capture and reflect light
- Dragon-leather wrapped hilt in deep navy, with dragon-wing crossguard
- Amber pommel gem emits a constant, gentle glow
- Glows with golden light in worthy hands
- Intensifies in presence of Tharizkorr corruption

**Dragon Order's Decree** (sealed scroll found with blade):
- Details requirements for wielding the blade:
  - Resist corruption's whispers
  - Honor past guardians' sacrifices
  - Protect innocents from the Rot
- Signed in blood by Commander Vigilantia, 347 Year of Heroes
