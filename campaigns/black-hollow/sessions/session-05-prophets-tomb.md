## Summary

Through magical interrogation, the party learns of a forest passage to the ruins. They solve the riddle to access the Prophet's tomb, defeat cultist guards, and make the critical decision not to wake the Prophet. Meanwhile, [[Black Hollow]] suffers a cultist attack that frees the previous prisoner, leading to harsh political consequences and a public execution.

## The Prophet

### Critical Decision

> [!important] Major Story Choice
> The party chose NOT to wake the Prophet, denying the cult access to their primary oracle.

- Found the Prophet in her sarcophagus (Dragon Cult origin)
- Described as a "crazed madre with powerful visions"
- **Party chose NOT to wake her**
- Secured the chamber and locked the door behind them
- Prevented cult from accessing their primary oracle

### The Knight's Investigation

**[[The Knight]] used Detect Thoughts on the Prophet:**

- Probed the Prophet's mind while she remained in the sarcophagus
- Made contact with **the Aboleth** through her consciousness
- Saw an ancient city and a misterious path on the woods
- **Entered into a pact with the Aboleth** to save his beloved
- **Failed WIS saving throw** during the interaction

> [!warning] DM Note
> **TO DECIDE:** What exactly did [[The Knight]] see during this mental contact? What were the terms of the pact? What are the consequences of the failed save?

### Strategic Implications

- Denying cultists access to prophetic guidance
- Prophet remains a potential resource or threat
- Knowledge of her location now with party
- Cult operations may be disrupted without visions
- **[[The Knight]] now has unknown connection to the Aboleth**
