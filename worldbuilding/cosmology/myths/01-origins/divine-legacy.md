## The Archfeys

- **Archfeys**: Lesser chaotic gods of Creation
- Guardians of the Verdant Dream
- Protectors of Viridiana's diminished form

The Archfeys, ancient and enigmatic beings of the Feywild, took it upon themselves to protect Viridiana. This decision was born from a mix of reverence, pragmatism, and ancient pacts:

- **Reverence**: Viridiana’s act of pure love resonates deeply with the Fey, who value emotion and the extremes of existence. They see her as the embodiment of sacrifice and the natural cycle.
- **Ancient Pact**: The Archfeys were bound to protect Viridiana by a covenant with her parents, Creation and Destruction, who tasked them with preserving her diminished form until she could restore herself—or until she is needed again.
- **Pragmatism**: The Archfeys understand that without Viridiana, the cycle of life and death would crumble, leaving only Chaos. Her existence is vital to the balance of all planes.

- **Symbiotic**: While the Archfeys protect Viridiana, they draw strength from the Verdant Dream. Her presence amplifies their power within the Feywild.
- **Guides, Not Masters**: Though diminished, Viridiana still holds a fragment of her divine Knowledge. The Archfeys often seek her guidance in matters of cosmic significance, but her answers are cryptic, spoken in riddles or through visions in the Dreamscapes.
- **Fragile Balance**: Not all Archfeys agree on their role as her guardians. Some view her as a burden or a threat, while others are wholly devoted to her safety.

## The Demons

- **Demons**: Lesser chaotic gods of Destruction
- Born from chaotic destruction
- Oppose the natural order
