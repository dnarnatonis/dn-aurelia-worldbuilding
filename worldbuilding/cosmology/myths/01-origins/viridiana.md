## The Birth of Nature

Born from the union of Creation and Destruction, Viridiana (Nature) shaped reality:

1. Formed the Elemental Planes from pure chaos
2. Created the Material Plane as a realm of cycles
3. Sacrificed her Will out of pure love to create mortal souls
4. Retreated to the Verdant Dream

## The Verdant Dream

The **Verdant Dream** is a lush, surreal plane within the Feywilds, a liminal space where time flows erratically and the natural world thrives in its most untamed and fantastical forms. This realm was created as a sanctuary for Viridiana after her self-sacrifice, shaped by the remnants of her Power and Knowledge, even though her Will was gone. It is an eternal spring, with towering crystalline trees, rivers of liquid light, and flowers that hum with the echoes of creation.

### Features

- **Nature's Heart**: At the center lies the Heart of Viridiana, a colossal tree imbued with her remaining Power and Knowledge. This tree is said to pulse with the energy of every soul she created.
- **Dreamscapes**: The plane manifests the dreams of mortals, reflecting their hopes, fears, and memories in vivid, ever-changing landscapes.
- **Eternal Guardians**: The Archfeys crafted sentinels known as Verdant Wardens, powerful constructs of root, stone, and living wood, to defend the sanctity of the realm.

### Key Points

- A sanctuary within the Feywild
- Protected by Archfeys
- Connected to mortal dreams and natural forces

## Impact on Mortals

The Verdant Dream has a mystical connection to the material plane. Mortals occasionally glimpse it in dreams, moments of inspiration, or deep communion with nature. Druids and rangers, in particular, feel Viridiana’s presence keenly, drawing power from the faint echoes of her Will. Some adventurers have even claimed to enter the Verdant Dream during near-death experiences or through ancient Fey gates.

### Cults and Worship

- **The Verdant Circle**: A secretive order of druids who worship Viridiana as the Mother of Souls. They believe she will one day restore her Will and bring balance to all creation.
- **The Seekers of the Dream**: Mortals who search for ways to enter the Verdant Dream, often through dangerous rituals or by forging pacts with Archfeys.
