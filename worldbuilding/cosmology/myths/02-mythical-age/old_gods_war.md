## The First Wars

The **Mythical Age** was the first epoch of the Material Plane, a time when the forces of Chaos still lingered and the balance of reality hung by a thread. The Old Gods, beings born from the edges of reality, sought to dominate this new creation. These entities had usurped the power of other Outer Gods, embodying concepts like Madness, Hunger, and Despair, twisted and alien forms of their original domains. They descended upon the Material Plane to twist it into their own image, disrupting the cycles that Viridiana had carefully crafted.

### The Old Gods and Their Threat

- Beings from reality's edge
- Usurped power from other Outer Gods
- Represented corrupted concepts (Madness, etc.)

The Old Gods were vast and incomprehensible, manifesting their Will in grotesque avatars that reshaped the world around them. Each Old God represented a perversion of the Outer Gods’ abstract concepts.

Their presence warped the Material Plane, turning it into a reflection of their own alien minds. Reality buckled under their Power as they bent the rules of existence, threatening to unravel the natural order.

#### Zhalath'ka, the Womb of Madness

This Old God spread mental chaos, warping minds and turning mortals into gibbering husks or loyal fanatics. Entire regions became dreamscapes of shifting realities.

#### Ulthrex, the Maw Eternal

The embodiment of insatiable Hunger. Its influence caused famine, parasitic plagues, and relentless consumption, devouring the essence of creation itself.

#### Nyvolos, the Shadow of Despair

This Old God wielded despair as a weapon, plunging civilizations into hopelessness and decay, sapping the strength of those who opposed it.
Their presence warped the Material Plane, turning it into a reflection of their own alien minds. Reality buckled under their Power as they bent the rules of existence, threatening to unravel the natural order.

### The First Sentient Races

To combat this existential threat, Viridiana forged the Dragons and Giants, each imbued with specific aspects of the elements and her divine Knowledge:

#### The Dragons

Born of Fire and Air, Dragons were creatures of immense power and intellect, embodying both destruction and wisdom. They wielded primal energy to cleanse the corruption brought by the Old Gods.
Each Dragon was tied to a specific elemental aspect, from blazing infernos to icy storms, serving as guardians of the Material Plane’s elemental balance.

#### The Giants

Born of Earth and Water, Giants were beings of incredible strength and resilience, serving as the physical bulwark against the forces of the Old Gods.
They were craftsmen and builders, tasked with creating fortresses, tools, and weapons to defend the natural world.

### The Mythical Wars

The wars of the Mythical Age were cataclysmic, spanning centuries and reshaping the Material Plane. Key events include:

#### The Elemental Reckoning

The Old Gods corrupted the Elemental Planes, turning fire into consuming infernos, water into endless floods, and air into storms of madness. The Dragons, tied to these planes, waged brutal battles to cleanse them. This was the first major victory, as the Dragons managed to restore balance to the elemental realms.

#### The Rise of the Giant-Kings

The Giants, united under the leadership of the Titan-King Azrelon, created the Pillars of Balance, massive structures infused with Viridiana’s remaining Power. These served as anchors to stabilize the Material Plane against the reality-warping influence of the Old Gods.

#### The Sundering of Zhalath'ka

The Dragons and Giants joined forces to slay Zhalath'ka, the Womb of Madness, in an epic battle at the heart of the Ebon Rift, a region warped beyond recognition. Though they succeeded, Zhalath'ka’s death scream fractured reality, leaving scars on the Material Plane where nightmares still seep through.

#### The Sealing of Ulthrex

Ulthrex, the Maw Eternal, could not be destroyed, as its essence was infinite. Instead, the Archfeys and Dragons combined their powers to imprison it in the Void Cairn, a massive structure hidden deep in the Astral Plane. Its hunger still echoes in the minds of mortals, inspiring acts of greed and gluttony.

### The Aftermath and the Mortal Legacy

The wars left the Material Plane scarred but victorious. The Old Gods were either destroyed, imprisoned, or driven to the edges of reality. However, their influence lingers:

#### Corrupted Lands

Certain regions remain cursed, twisted by the power of the Old Gods. These areas are avoided or inhabited by cults seeking to revive their masters.

#### Mortals' Burden

As Viridiana’s Will was spent, mortals inherited free will and the responsibility to maintain balance in the Material Plane. Heroes and champions now take up the mantle once carried by Dragons and Giants.

## Modern Echoes of the Mythical Age

The Old Gods, while defeated, are not gone. Cults dedicated to their return plot in secret, and their lingering essence still corrupts mortals. Relics of the Mythical Age, such as the Heartshards of Zhalath'ka or the Chains of Ulthrex, remain potent and dangerous.

Meanwhile, Dragons and Giants have faded in influence. While some still roam the world, their numbers are few, and their power diminished. Mortals now stand as the primary line of defense against the remnants of the Old Gods.
