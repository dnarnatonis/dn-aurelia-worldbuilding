Created by Viridiana to manage portions of her Power over the Material Plane:

## The Divine Eight

### 1. Zaralon

- **Domains**: Fire, Courage, Passion, War
- **Appearance**: Towering figure wreathed in eternal flame
- **Symbol**: Flame encased in circle
- **Names**:
  - Elvish: Caelathar ("Burning Star")
  - Dwarvish: Thargrund ("Forge Flame")
  - Halfling: Blazefor ("Brave Fire")

### 2. Astrael

- **Domains**: Air, Storms, Stars, Wisdom
- **Appearance**: Being of radiant light and swirling winds
- **Symbol**: Starburst surrounded by winds
- **Names**:
  - Elvish: Eryndor ("Sky Warden")
  - Dwarvish: Zarakul ("Star Hammer")
  - Halfling: Skystar ("Bright Sky")

### 3. Sylvara

- **Domains**: Life, Cycles, Fertility, Growth
- **Appearance**: Motherly figure adorned with natural elements
- **Symbol**: Blooming flower within crescent moon
- **Names**:
  - Elvish: Lothiriel ("Blossom Lady")
  - Dwarvish: Thalvora ("Stone's Bloom")
  - Halfling: Greenglen ("Life of the Valley")

### 4. Thalvok

- **Domains**: Beasts, Instinct, Wild Nature
- **Appearance**: Shape-shifting beast-like figure
- **Symbol**: Clawed paw within circle
- **Names**:
  - Elvish: Rhuvenar ("Guardian of the Wild")
  - Dwarvish: Hargrom ("Beastlord")
  - Halfling: Wildkin ("Friend of Beasts")

### 5. Morthys

- **Domains**: Death, Passage, Endings
- **Appearance**: Serene figure in black and silver
- **Symbol**: Skeletal hand holding hourglass
- **Names**:
  - Elvish: Faelyth ("Silent One")
  - Dwarvish: Durhal ("Stone's Rest")
  - Halfling: Nightshade ("Gentle Reaper")

### 6. Voryn

- **Domains**: Shadow, Secrets, Subterfuge
- **Appearance**: Shifting, shadowy figure
- **Symbol**: Closed eye in shadow wisps
- **Names**:
  - Elvish: Ulthryn ("Hidden Whisper")
  - Dwarvish: Thazrak ("Deep Veil")
  - Halfling: Duskmurk ("Quiet Shadow")

### 7. Elyndra

- **Domains**: Light, Order, Justice
- **Appearance**: Radiant figure in golden armor
- **Symbol**: Blazing sun over open book
- **Names**:
  - Elvish: Lirael ("Beacon of Order")
  - Dwarvish: Anvarn ("Golden Flame")
  - Halfling: Sunbright ("Guiding Light")

### 8. Khorvyn

- **Domains**: Earth, Metals, Gems, Transmutation
- **Appearance**: Figure of stone with gem and gold veins
- **Symbol**: Split mountain with glowing core
- **Names**:
  - Elvish: Narvenor ("Shaper of Stone")
  - Dwarvish: Duragoth ("Mountain Father")
  - Halfling: Stonehearth ("Earth's Forge")
