## Friendship Between the Empire and the Western Dwarven Clans

---

### **Who**  

The **Ironshield Clan** and the **Stonefist Clan** are the two Dwarven clans that share a strong alliance with the human empire. Known for their craftsmanship and military might, these clans have been close allies of the empire since its early days.

---

### **What**  

The alliance is built on mutual respect and cooperation. The Dwarves provide exceptional weapons, armor, and engineering expertise, while the empire offers protection, trade routes, and agricultural resources.

---

### **When**  

The friendship began during the reign of the first Aasimar Empress, **Serenai the Radiant**, after the **Battle of Keldor’s Pass**, where the Ironshield and Stonefist clans aided the empire in defending against a massive orc invasion.

---

### **Where**  

The clans are located in the **Ironspire Mountains**, along the western border of the empire. Their fortress-cities, **Kaz-Garond** (Ironshield) and **Thal-Karrek** (Stonefist), sit near strategic mountain passes, making them key to the region's defense.

---

### **Why**  

The alliance was forged out of necessity:

- The Dwarves needed safe trade routes and a reliable source of grain and supplies.
- The empire needed allies to secure its western border and access to the Dwarves' superior metallurgy and engineering.

---

### **How**  

The partnership was formalized through the **Pact of Iron**, a treaty that outlined mutual defense agreements, trade rights, and cultural exchanges. Key aspects include:

- A **permanent Dwarven embassy** in the imperial capital.
- Annual joint military exercises to maintain border security.
- Shared projects like the **Ironroad**, a fortified trade route connecting the Dwarven fortresses to imperial cities.
