## Modern Cosmology

### The Cycle of Existence

- Material Plane maintains balance through natural cycles
- Mortals possess free will from Viridiana's sacrifice
- Elements remain in constant flux between Creation and Destruction

### Remnants of the Past

- Old God cults persist
- Dragons and Giants diminished but present
- Verdant Dream continues to influence nature
