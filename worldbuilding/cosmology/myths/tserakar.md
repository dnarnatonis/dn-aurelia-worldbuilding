# **Tserakar - The Festival Lord**

Tserakar is a **Lesser God** under the dominion of **Ulmathar**, the Moon God. Known as the **Lord of Crimson Revelry**, he oversees sacred rites and divine trials that test mortals' worthiness of forbidden knowledge. His most notable manifestation is the **Crimson Festival**, an otherworldly competition held under the blood-streaked moon.

---

## **Origins and Nature**

- **Divine Purpose:** Tasked with testing those who seek cosmic knowledge
- **Domain:** Trials, Revelry, and Divine Tests
- **Manifestation:** The Crimson Moon's herald
- **Sacred Role:** Curator of forbidden ceremonies and keeper of lunar mysteries

---

## **The Crimson Festival**

Tserakar's greatest work is the Crimson Festival, a divine trial that serves multiple purposes:

- **Tests worthiness** of those seeking lunar knowledge
- **Harvests secrets** from participants through their trials
- **Strengthens** the Moon God's influence through ritual and sacrifice
- **Reveals hidden truths** through carefully orchestrated challenges

---

## **Divine Form and Presence**

### **Appearance**
- Cloaked in flowing robes of liquid shadow
- Wears a mask of silver and onyx etched with lunar sigils
- Form suggests divine nature: elongated limbs marked with moonlight
- Eyes pool with silvery radiance, reflecting Ulmathar's gaze

### **Personality**
- Speaks in riddles and cosmic truths
- Exhibits theatrical flair in interactions
- Maintains an air of both charm and menace
- Shows both wisdom and cruel calculation

---

## **Role in the Divine Hierarchy**

### **Relationship with Ulmathar**
- Serves as the Moon God's herald and trial-master
- Maintains the boundary between worthy and unworthy seekers
- Orchestrates tests of knowledge and will
- Guards ancient lunar ceremonies

### **Interaction with Other Lesser Gods**
- Works alongside **Voryn** in matters of shadow and secrecy
- Collaborates with **Veshka** to test and challenge mortals
- Maintains diplomatic ties with other divine courts

---

## **The Festival's True Purpose**

The Crimson Festival serves as more than mere entertainment or trial:

- **Knowledge Gathering:** Each participant's journey reveals secrets
- **Power Enhancement:** Ritual energies strengthen lunar influence
- **Divine Selection:** Identifies those worthy of lunar mysteries
- **Cosmic Balance:** Maintains the hierarchy between mortal and divine

---

## **Sacred Sites and Artifacts**

- **The Crimson Court:** His divine realm where the Festival is planned
- **The Mask of Phases:** His sacred artifact that reveals hidden truths
- **Festival Grounds:** Temporary spaces sanctified for his trials
- **Blood Moon Altars:** Places where his influence is strongest

---

## **Signs and Manifestations**

- **Red-tinged moonlight** heralds his attention
- **Shadow patterns** form mysterious symbols
- **Dreams of masked figures** dancing under crimson skies
- **Unexplained music** echoing in empty places

---

## **Current Influence**

- **Secret societies** seek to recreate his festivals
- **Moon priests** invoke his name during lunar ceremonies
- **Knowledge seekers** sometimes petition for his trials
- **His influence grows** strongest during blood moons
