# **The Moon God – Ulmathar / Ithilthar / Rhevas**  

The **Moon God** is a **Greater God** associated with **secrets, hidden knowledge, and the arcane**, one of the Outer Gods who shapes reality through mystery and hidden truth. Unlike the Lesser Gods created by Viridiana, Ulmathar exists as a fundamental force of cosmic secrecy.

- **Abyssal Name:** **Ulmathar** (*The Silent Witness, The Pale Watcher*)  
- **Elvish Name:** **Ithilthar** (*The Silver Veil, Keeper of the Lost Words*)  
- **Celestial Name:** **Rhevas** (*The Veiled Moon*)
- **Domain:** **The Moon, Secrets, Forbidden Knowledge, Illusions, and Destiny**  
- **Manifestation:** The Moon itself, ever-changing yet eternal
- **Lesser Gods in Service:** Voryn (Shadow), Veshka (Deception)

---

## **1. Role in Cosmic Order**  

- **Guardian of Secrets**: Maintains the balance between knowledge and mystery
- **Keeper of Hidden Paths**: Controls access to forbidden truths and ancient wisdom
- **Cosmic Observer**: Witnesses all but reveals little, maintaining the veil between truth and illusion
- **Some believe he predates Viridiana**, existing as one of the original Outer Gods

### **Divine Philosophy & Nature**  

- **The Moon sees all but speaks not.** *To know is power; to reveal is to weaken yourself.*  
- **Illusions are not lies, but veils that protect knowledge from the unworthy.**  
- **Destiny is a tapestry of hidden threads—only those who understand its patterns can weave their own fate.**  
- **Knowledge must be earned**, for gifts freely given lose their value.

---

## **2. Relationship with the High Elves**  

- **The High Elves recognized Ulmathar's power**, seeing in the moon a reflection of their own pursuit of knowledge
- **Queen Elathis Araenor** forged an alliance with him during the Elven Schism
- Their devotion to arcane secrets aligned with his nature as an Outer God
- This alliance forever changed their culture, leading them toward isolation and the hoarding of knowledge

---

## **3. Divine Influence**  

- **The Moon's Phases** reflect his shifting nature and influence
- **Lunar Magic** draws directly from his power
- **Dreams and Prophecies** often come under his domain
- **The Boundaries** between truth and illusion are his to maintain

---

## **4. Sacred Sites & Artifacts**  

- **Ancient lunar temples** serve as repositories of forbidden knowledge
- **The Silver Veil**, his most powerful artifact, grants visions of destiny
- **Moonwells**, scattered across the world, serve as conduits for his power
- **The Lost City** of the High Elves contains his most sacred texts

---

## **5. Divine Hierarchy and Servants**

### **Lesser Gods**

- **Veshka**, the Shadowbound Trickster, maintains the boundary between knowledge and ignorance
- **Tserakar**, the Festival Lord, oversees trials of worthiness and lunar ceremonies

### **Divine Agents**

- **The Pale Lady**, a manifestation of lunar power
- **Moon Seers**, mortals blessed with his visions
- **Shadow Weavers**, practitioners of his most secret arts

---

## **6. Current Influence**

- **The Imperial Church** views his worship with suspicion
- **Arcane practitioners** seek his blessing in secret
- **The Cabal** claims connection to his hidden knowledge
- **His influence grows** strongest during lunar events

---

## **7. Signs and Omens**

- **Unusual lunar phenomena** often herald his attention
- **Dreams of silver light** mark those he watches
- **Shadows moving against the light** suggest his presence
- **Knowledge found in unexpected places** may be his gift—or his test
