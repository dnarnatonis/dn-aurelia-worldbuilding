# **Veshka - The Shadowbound Lesser God**

Veshka, the **Shadowbound Trickster**, is a Lesser God who serves **Ulmathar** (known in the Elvish tongue as **Ithilthar**). Born from the Moon God's shadow during the Age of Balance, Veshka embodies the more subtle and deceptive aspects of Ulmathar's divine domain, serving as both a guardian of forbidden knowledge and an agent of cosmic interference.

---

## **Origins and Nature**

- **Shadow Incarnate:** A fragment of Ulmathar's own essence, given form and purpose
- **Divine Purpose:** Tasked with preventing the disruption of cosmic secrets and maintaining the boundary between mortal understanding and divine knowledge
- **Domain:** Shadows, deception, and the spaces between truth and lies
- **Form:** A divine entity that appears as a shifting shadow, capable of assuming various forms to suit its needs

---

## **Role in the Divine Hierarchy**

As a Lesser God under Ulmathar's dominion, Veshka serves specific functions in the cosmic order:

- **Guardian of Secrets:** Protects forbidden knowledge from those deemed unworthy
- **Cosmic Disruptor:** Prevents mortals from uncovering truths that could threaten the divine order
- **Shadow's Voice:** Acts as Ulmathar's agent in matters requiring subtlety and deception
- **Dream Walker:** Moves through mortal dreams, planting seeds of doubt or misdirection

### **Methods of Influence**

1. **Knowledge Keeper:** Obscures dangerous truths through illusion and misdirection
2. **Dream Weaver:** Uses dreams to guide or mislead those who seek forbidden knowledge
3. **Bargain Maker:** Offers knowledge in exchange for service or sacrifice
4. **Fate Twister:** Subtly alters the course of events to maintain cosmic secrets

---

## **Personality**

- **Demeanor:** Charming and polite, yet unsettling
- **Speech:** Speaks in riddles and half-truths
- **Nature:** Shows both empathy and predatory cunning
- **Motivation:** Driven by unwavering loyalty to the Moon God

---

## **Encountering Veshka**

### **Common Scenarios**

1. **The Tempter:** Appears offering solutions to desperate situations
2. **The Shadow:** Manifests as an observer or subtle manipulator
3. **The Bargainer:** Presents deals too good to refuse

### **Warning Signs**

- Unexplained disappearances of gifted individuals
- Strange shadows moving against the light
- Dreams becoming increasingly vivid and prophetic
- Whispered promises in the dark
