The first language, spoken by the Primordial Forces and elemental beings.

## Origins

- Born from the first utterances of Creation and Destruction
- Contains raw power within its syllables
- Four major dialects corresponding to elemental planes

## Characteristics

### Sound System

- Harsh consonants and resonant vowels
- Tonal elements that carry elemental power
- Each syllable represents a fundamental force

### Dialects

1. **Ignan** (Fire Primordial)
   - Sharp, crackling sounds
   - Ascending tones
   - Heavy emphasis on 'r' and 'z' sounds

2. **Aquan** (Water Primordial)
   - Flowing, liquid consonants
   - Undulating tones
   - Emphasis on 'm' and 'l' sounds

3. **Terran** (Earth Primordial)
   - Deep, resonant sounds
   - Steady, unchanging tones
   - Strong 'k' and 'th' sounds

4. **Auran** (Air Primordial)
   - Breathy, whistling sounds
   - Variable pitch
   - Prominent 'h' and 's' sounds

## Modern Usage

- Primarily used in elemental magic
- Sacred language in ancient rituals
- Known by scholars and powerful spellcasters
