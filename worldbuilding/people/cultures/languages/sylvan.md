The language of nature and the Fey, born from Viridiana's shaping of reality.

## Origins

- Created when Viridiana formed the Material Plane
- Evolved within the Verdant Dream
- Shaped by the first Archfeys

## Characteristics

### Sound System

- Melodic and fluid
- Natural rhythms mimicking growth cycles
- Complex harmony between consonants and vowels

### Grammar

- Verb-centric structure emphasizing action and change
- No distinction between past and future, only cycles
- Words change meaning based on natural seasons

### Notable Features

1. **Living Words**
   - Words grow and change meaning like plants
   - Pronunciation shifts with seasons
   - Meanings evolve over centuries

2. **Nature Bonds**
   - Speaking certain words can influence plant growth
   - Animal communication elements
   - Weather-responsive expressions

3. **Dream Logic**
   - Paradoxical structures that make sense in Fey realms
   - Words that can only be spoken in moonlight
   - Phrases that create minor magical effects

## Modern Usage

- Common among druids and fey creatures
- Used in nature magic and plant-speaking
- Preserved by Archfey courts and ancient groves
