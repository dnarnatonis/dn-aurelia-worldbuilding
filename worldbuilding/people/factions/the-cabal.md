# The Cabal

The **Cabal** is a **shadowy, Illuminati-like society** embedded within the highest levels of **imperial power**, influencing politics, religion, and commerce from the shadows. They are **not a single unified force**, but a **network of secretive elites**, each pursuing their **own ambitions under the shared goal of reshaping the empire to their advantage.**  

---

## **Structure of the Cabal**  

- **Membership is exclusive**—only powerful nobles, influential clergy, high-ranking military officers, and master merchants are invited.  
- **Divided into cells**, with each faction pursuing different aspects of the Cabal's overarching agenda.  
- **Each member is identified only by a codename**, ensuring secrecy even within the group.  
- The **Cabal's true goal is unknown**, even among its members—some seek knowledge, others control, and a few **chase something far darker.**  

### **The Inner Circle**

- **The Serpent** - A high-ranking member of the Harmonic Order, manipulating church doctrine
- **The Crown** - A noble close to Empress Celestara II, influencing succession politics
- **The Shadow** - Master of the empire's spy network, trading in secrets and blackmail
- **The Merchant** - Controls banking houses and trade routes across multiple duchies

---

## **Notable Cabal Figures**  

### **The Gravedigger** *(Agent Focused on Black Hollow)*  

- **A noblewoman of unknown identity**, **obsessed with unearthing ancient secrets.**  
- **Believes Black Hollow hides something of immense power**, buried beneath its ruins.  
- **Employs mercenaries, informants, and spies**, never appearing in person.  
- **Her methods are subtle—bribes, whispers, disappearances.**  
- **Some say she does not seek power, but knowledge lost to time—no matter the cost.**  

### **Other Known Cabal Figures:**  

- **The Archivist** – A **scholar-priest** who controls hidden libraries of forbidden knowledge.  
- **The Coinmaster** – A **merchant prince** who manipulates trade, controls banking, and funds wars.  
- **The Black Lance** – A **high-ranking general** with a hidden army, waiting for the right moment.  
- **The Mirror** – A **master infiltrator**, who might sit in the imperial court itself.  

---

## **Current Objectives**

### **Political Manipulation**

- Influencing the imperial succession crisis
- Placing agents in key positions within ducal courts
- Undermining potential rivals to their power

### **Occult Research**

- Searching for artifacts from the Age of Heroes
- Studying the corruption beneath Black Hollow
- Collecting ancient texts in Abyssal and other dead languages

### **Economic Control**

- Manipulating trade routes and banking systems
- Funding proxy wars between rival duchies
- Controlling the flow of rare resources

---

## **Methods of Operation**

### **Recruitment**

- Identifying potential members through careful observation
- Testing loyalty through increasingly sensitive tasks
- Using both threats and rewards to ensure compliance

### **Communication**

- Messages encoded in seemingly innocent correspondence
- Dead drops in libraries and temples
- Use of compromised merchant caravans

### **Enforcement**

- Blackmail and economic pressure
- "Accidents" for those who threaten exposure
- Manipulation of local authorities

---

## **Known Fronts**

- **The Golden Quill Society** - A scholarly organization serving as a recruitment ground
- **The Merchant's Guild of Celestra** - Used for moving agents and resources
- **The Order of the Eternal Flame** - A religious sect providing cover for occult research
- **The Imperial Historical Society** - Access to ancient ruins and artifacts

---

## **Current Schemes**

1. **Project Inheritance**
   - Manipulating the imperial succession
   - Placing agents close to potential heirs
   - Gathering compromising information on rival claimants

2. **The Black Hollow Initiative**
   - Investigating ancient ruins for powerful artifacts
   - Monitoring supernatural activity in the region
   - Controlling information about local discoveries

3. **Operation Golden Chain**
   - Creating a shadow banking network
   - Manipulating currency values
   - Funding covert operations through legitimate businesses
